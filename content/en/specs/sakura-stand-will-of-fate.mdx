<Section icon={Target} title="How to Obtain the Will of Fate">

Obtaining the **sakura stand will of fate** has historically been one of the most difficult challenges for collectors. Unlike Stands that can be farmed through specific NPC quests or common arrows, the Will of Fate relies almost entirely on RNG (Random Number Generation) within limited-time event items. 

The primary vehicle for this item is the **Bag of Presents**. These bags are typically distributed during winter holiday events. Because the drop rate is set at a staggering 0.06%, players often have to open hundreds, if not thousands, of bags to see the item appear in their inventory.
