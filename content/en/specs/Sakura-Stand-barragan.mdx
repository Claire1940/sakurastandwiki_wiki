<Section icon={Target} title="The Aizen Sosuke Questline: Step-by-Step">

The encounter with **Sakura Stand barragan** is part of a much larger, 14-step questline to obtain the Aizen Sosuke specification. This is currently considered one of the most intensive grinds in the game, requiring players to obtain multiple other specs just to satisfy Aizen's demands.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate the Quest">
    Obtain the Soul Reaper spec (20% chance from killing Hollows with Demon Slayer Initiate). Go to Las Noches in Sakura Mundo and speak to Aizen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Five Swords">
    Aizen will demand five specific swords. You must obtain and bring him the Haunted Sword, Split Soul Katana, Ten-Twee Blade, Mimicry, and Nichirin Katana.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Craft the Hogyoku">
    Gather 5 Hogyoku Fragments, 5 Cracked Masks, and 5 Shattered Hollow Souls to craft an Incomplete Hogyoku. Use 10 more Shattered Hollow Souls to complete it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The First Boss Trial">
    Deliver the Complete Hogyoku. Aizen will then task you with killing 30 Hollows, 5 Menos, 2 Adjuchas, and finally, your first kill of **Sakura Stand barragan**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Strategies for Barragan">

Fighting **Sakura Stand barragan** requires a specific approach due to his unique debuffs. Unlike standard NPCs, Barragan utilizes "Respira" mechanics that distort the player's UI and controls. The most notable effect is the inverted camera, which flips your directional inputs and makes movement difficult.

| Attack Name | Effect | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Inversion** | Flips camera and movement controls | Practice moving with reversed WASD keys |
| **Screen Break** | Visual distortion/static on screen | Rely on sound cues and health bar positioning |
| **AoE Burst** | High damage radial explosion | Maintain distance or use iframe abilities |
| **Soul Decay** | Passive damage over time (DoT) | Use high-sustain specs like Ronin for the kill |

Using a spec like Ronin is highly recommended for the actual combat phase because its percentage-based damage scales well against Barragan's high HP pool. However, remember that you must switch back to the Soul Reaper spec to talk to Aizen and progress the quest steps once the boss is defeated.

<Callout type="success" title="Kill Confirmation">
In the quest log, Barragan is explicitly called the "Vasto Lord." Do not confuse him with standard Vasto Lord mobs; only the boss kill counts toward the Aizen progression.
</Callout>

</Section>
