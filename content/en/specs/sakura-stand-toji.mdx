<Section icon={Target} title="Required Items and Boss Drops">

To evolve into the full Toji spec, you must first collect three specific legendary items. These items are exclusively dropped by the Toji Boss, which can be summoned in the main map area. Farming these items can be time-consuming due to the boss's difficult AI and the RNG nature of the drops.

| Item Name | Description | Drop Rarity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Inverted Spear of Heaven | A powerful cursed tool for dashing and piercing. | Legendary |
| Chain of a Thousand Miles | Used for pulling enemies and extending combos. | Legendary |
| Soul Splitter Katana | The primary weapon for high-damage M1 strings. | Legendary |
| Heavenly Restriction | A status effect required for the final reset. | Rare Status |

The boss fight itself is a test of endurance. The Toji Boss utilizes many of the same skills you will eventually unlock, including a lethal teleportation move and a conical cleave that deals significant damage. If you die during the fight, the boss will instantly begin regenerating health, which can reset minutes of progress.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
The Heavenly Restriction status is a temporary buff obtained from the boss. You must have all three items in your inventory while this status is active to successfully evolve. If you reset without the items or the status, you will not obtain the spec.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Moveset and Combat Mechanics">

Once you have unlocked **sakura stand toji**, you gain access to a moveset designed for aggressive, fast-paced combat. His skills focus on closing the gap between him and the opponent, making it impossible for ranged users to stay safe for long.

| Keybind | Skill Name | Effect / Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| M1 | Katana String | Fast melee combo that pulls enemies closer. |
| Z | Flash Dash | Zips around the map; can be used multiple times. |
| T | Sword Dash | A forward lunge that pierces through blocks. |
| R | Conical Cleave | A wide-range slash that acts as a projectile. |
| G | Teleport Strike | Teleports behind the target for a surprise hit. |

Toji's mobility is his greatest asset. The Z skill allows you to "zip" across large distances, making it the best movement tool in the game for dodging ultimates like Gojo's Hollow Purple. In combat, your goal should be to use the Chain of a Thousand Miles (often integrated into M1s or specific skills) to reel in opponents, followed by a high-damage Katana string.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival and Meta Placement">

In the current 2026 meta of Sakura Stand, Toji sits firmly in the S-tier for skilled players but drops significantly for beginners. His effectiveness is entirely dependent on the player's ability to read the opponent's movements. Because he lacks the massive health pools of stands like Star Platinum or the defensive hax of Gojo, Toji must be played with a "hit and run" mentality.

| Stat | Rating | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Damage | ★★★★★ | Exceptional burst and sustained DPS. |

| Defense | ★★☆☆☆ | Very fragile; requires manual dodging. |
| Difficulty | ★★★★☆ | High skill ceiling for effective use. |

When facing Toji, opponents will often try to use AOE skills to catch you during your dashes. To counter this, use the "Throughout Heaven" and "Throughout Earth" variations of your skills to stay unpredictable. The spec's ability to pull enemies toward you is particularly effective against runners who try to reset the fight.

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Meta Note">
Toji is highly susceptible to "one-combo" specs. If you get caught in a stun-lock, it is likely game over. Prioritize learning the startup animations of other top-tier specs to know when to zip away.
</Callout>
