<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Unlocking Starrk">

Following the correct sequence is vital. If you miss an NPC interaction in the Dark Alley or fail to collect a specific fragment, you may find yourself stuck in Hueco Mundo without a clear path forward. Follow these steps to secure the Coyote Starrk Spec.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate Soul Reaper Tasks">
    Start by interacting with Urahara Kisuke or related Soul Reaper NPCs in the main world. You must complete the initial Substitute Shinigami tasks to gain access to the Hueco Mundo portal. Ensure you have farmed enough Hollows in the forest areas first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter Hueco Mundo and Las Noches">
    Locate the portal to Hueco Mundo. Once inside, navigate toward the massive structure of Las Noches. This area serves as the hub for the Espada-related quests. You will need to speak with Aizen or his subordinates to trigger the "Starrk Route" flags.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm Hollow-Family Enemies">
    The core of the Starrk progression involves the "Hollow-family" kill count. You must hunt Gillians and Adjuchas within the desert. These enemies have a chance to drop Hogyoku Fragments, which are necessary for the later stages of the Bleach Spec lines.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat the Hueco Mundo Bosses">
    To progress the sword collection requirements, you must clear encounters with bosses like Nnoitra Gilga. These fights are mechanically intensive and often require a team or a highly optimized solo build. The drops from these bosses are the final keys to the Starrk Spec.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Finalize the Coyote Spec">
    Once all kill counts and item requirements are met, return to the specific quest NPC located near the Las Noches throne room. Exchange your collected materials to finally obtain the **sakura stand starrk** ability.
  </Step>
</Steps>
