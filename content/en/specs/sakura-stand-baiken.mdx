<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Obtainment Path">

Unlocking the **sakura stand baiken** spec involves a series of interconnected quests. You cannot skip steps, and you must follow the dialogue prompts carefully to ensure the game flags your character as "ready" for the next trial.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire the Haunted Sword">
    First, obtain the Anubis stand. This is typically done through standard Arrows or specific quests. Once you have Anubis, ensure you have reached the required Mastery level by using the stand in combat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Bounty Poster Hunt">
    Head to the Shop and look near the "Auddy with you" capsule. You are looking for a physical **Bounty Poster**. It has a 20% chance to appear every 5 minutes. Pick it up to initiate the questline.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Find the Wandering Samurai">
    Locate the Baiken NPC. She has multiple spawn points across the map. Once found, she will give you the **Prove Yourself** quest, which involves high-intensity combat trials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Trial of Combat">
    Complete the following objectives using Anubis: Kill 5 players, deal 2,000 total damage, and successfully land the Anubis **Y** key attack on players 9 times.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Meeting with Kuzma">
    After proving yourself, Baiken will send you to find "That Man" (Kuzma/All For One). Ensure your Anubis is unsheathed when talking to him. This step is where Mastery 4 is strictly checked.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Defeat the Gears">
    Return to Baiken. She will teleport you to a private arena. You must defeat three mechanical hounds known as "Gears." They are not exceptionally strong, but they can overwhelm you if you don't use your blocks effectively.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Arena Combat">

<Section icon={Swords} title="Baiken Moveset and Combat Analysis">

Once you have completed the trials and received the spare sword from Baiken, your character will transform. The **sakura stand baiken** spec replaces your Anubis moveset with a versatile kit featuring swords, pistols, and even mechanical chains.

| Key | Move Name | Damage | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Q | Heavy | 25 | A forward energy beam with high knockback |
| E | Tatami | 28 | Stomps a mat that flings enemies upward |
| R | Rifle | Variable | Long-range projectile for poking |
| Y | Chain Lash | Moderate | Pulls enemies toward you for combos |
| G | Secret Art | 140 | Massive cinematic finisher (High CD) |
| J | Taki | Utility | A specialized movement/attack skill |
| H | Metto Kush | Utility | Tactical repositioning move |

The core of Baiken's gameplay revolves around the **Chain Lash (Y)**. This move allows you to pull a distant enemy directly into your melee range, setting them up for a **Tatami (E)** launch or a **Heavy (Q)** blast.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Strategy and Meta Positioning">

In the current 2026 meta of Sakura Stand, Baiken is considered an "A-Tier" spec. While she lacks the sheer destructive power of some Mythic-tier stands, her utility and "catch" potential are unparalleled. To play her effectively, you must master the timing of her parries and the travel speed of her Rifle (R) shots.

**Key Strategic Points:**
- **Resource Management**: Baiken's skills have moderate cooldowns. Avoid "spamming" the Y skill, as missing it leaves you vulnerable to ranged attackers.
- **Environmental Awareness**: Use the Tatami mat near edges or walls to trap opponents and prevent them from escaping your follow-up attacks.
- **The Secret Art**: Use the G skill only when you are certain it will land, preferably after a stun. It deals 140 damage, which can end most fights instantly if the opponent's health is already mid-range.
