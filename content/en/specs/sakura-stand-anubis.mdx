<Section icon={Target} title="How to Obtain Base Anubis: The Haunted Sword">

To start your journey with **sakura stand anubis**, you must first be "Standless." The process involves locating a specific NPC and completing a series of trials to prove your worthiness to the blade.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Hika">

  <Step num="2" title="Complete the Haunted Sword Quest">
    You will need to perform various tasks, including dealing a specific amount of damage, taking damage, and successfully parrying attacks. Use the training dummies or low-level mobs to farm these stats quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Use the Haunted Sword">
    Once the quest is complete, Hika will grant you the Haunted Sword item. Ensure you are Standless, then use the item from your inventory to equip the Anubis Stand.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Base Anubis Move Set and Mechanics">

The base version of **sakura stand anubis** is a formidable tool in the right hands. It relies heavily on stuns and its unique "Sheathe/Unsheathe" mechanic to catch opponents in infinite-loop potential.

| Key | Move Name | Description | Damage/Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Q** | Summon | Unique sheathe/unsheathe animation with a glowing blade | Utility |
| **E** | Slicing Barrage | A sphere of slashes that stuns the opponent | 20 DMG / 3s Stun |
| **R** | Instant Slash | A quick, single horizontal slash | 12 DMG / 2s Stun |
| **T** | Single Strike | A forward dash followed by a powerful strike | 16-27 DMG |
| **Y** | Light Speed Attack | Throws the blade and teleports behind the target | 16 DMG / 2s Stun |
| **G** | Extended Piercing | A long-range poke that interrupts enemy actions | 14 DMG / 2s Stun |
| **H** | Awakening | Summons an ominous aura, boosting all move damage | 45s Duration |

<Callout type="warning" title="Awakening Penalty">
While the "It'll only take one hit..." awakening (H) provides a massive damage boost, it deals 75 damage to the user once the 45-second duration ends. Use it only when you are certain you can finish the fight.
</Callout>

The **Sharp Blade** passive is the core of your pressure. Even if an opponent blocks your **Instant Slash** or **Slicing Barrage**, they will still take chip damage and be forced into a defensive posture, allowing you to follow up with a guard-breaking **Single Strike**.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="True Anubis Move Set and Advanced Combos">

True Anubis is significantly faster than its predecessor. It introduces a "Combo Rank" system (displayed as letters like D, C, B, A, S) that determines the effectiveness of your skills.

**Key Differences in True Anubis:**
- **M1 Speed**: Your basic attacks are much faster and can be chained into a "Cleave Combo."
- **Barrage Enhancement**: The E-move barrage is more compact and deals damage faster.
- **Calm Mind (S-Rank)**: When you reach an "S" rank in your combo meter, you can activate specialized versions of your skills that have different properties and visuals.
- **Aura**: True Anubis features a permanent visual aura that signifies its legendary status.

<Callout type="tip" title="S-Rank Mastery">
To reach S-rank quickly, alternate between your M1 strings and your G (Extended Piercing) poke. Once S-rank is achieved, your skills gain additional range and reduced start-up frames.
</Callout>

| Skill Type | Change from Base | Combat Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **M1 Strings** | Faster, 5-hit combo | High; leads into Cleave |
| **Cleave Combo** | New follow-up | Excellent for finishing |
| **Dash / Mobility** | Reduced cooldown | Top-tier for chasing |
| **Ultimates** | Higher scaling | Game-ending potential |
