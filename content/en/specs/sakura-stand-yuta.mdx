<Section icon={Target} title="Best Combos and Strategies">

To maximize your efficiency with the **sakura stand yuta** spec, you must practice reliable combo strings. Relying on single, unchained attacks will leave you vulnerable to counters from experienced opponents.

### The Bread-and-Butter Combo
This sequence is highly effective in both PvP and PvE scenarios. It deals substantial damage while keeping the opponent locked in hitstun:

1. Perform 3 basic melee attacks (M1, M1, M1).
2. Activate **Cursed Speech** (X) to freeze the opponent in place.
3. Dash forward and execute **Katana Slash** (C) to deal rapid cutting damage.
4. Immediately follow up with **Pure Love** (Z) while the opponent is falling to maximize damage.

### Defensive Playstyle
If you find yourself on the defensive, use Yuta's mobility to space out your opponent. Keep your distance, bait out their primary escape options, and punish their recovery frames with a quick Cursed Speech setup.
