To begin the quest path, you must participate in the Holy Grail raid. This is a battle royale-style mode where the last player standing wins the opportunity to make a wish.

<Section icon={Target} title="The Epic of Gilgamesh Quest Guide">

Once you have secured the initial items from the Holy Grail, you must find the NPC located near the highest peak (often referred to as the Hippo area) to start "The Epic of Gilgamesh" quest.

| Objective | Requirement | Best Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Kill Dio** | 1 Time | Use high-damage specs with stuns. |
| **Kill Toji** | 1 Time | Keep distance and bait his dash. |
| **Kill a Player** | Use Astolfo | Find a willing friend or win a fair duel. |
| **Win a 1v1** | 1 Match | Use your most comfortable PvP spec. |
| **Emote Usage** | `/e makingshape` (20x) | Can be done quickly in any safe zone. |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate the Quest">
    Travel to the highest peak and speak with the NPC to receive the quest "The Epic of Gilgamesh." Ensure you have the Holy Grail items in your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Boss Hunting">
    Focus on Dio and Toji first. These bosses can be buggy, so if they don't spawn correctly, you may need to server hop or wait for a fresh instance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Social and Combat Goals">
    Perform the `/e makingshape` emote 20 times. Afterward, head to the 1v1 arena to secure your player kill and match victory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Integration">
    Return to the quest giver with the completed objectives to receive the "Epic of Gilgamesh" rock item, which allows you to finalize the spec.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Moveset Analysis and Combat Data">

The **sakura stand gilgamesh** spec is defined by its "Gate of Babylon" aesthetic, utilizing summoned weapons to overwhelm opponents from any distance.

| Key | Move Name | Type | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Q** | King's Authority | Ranged/Auto | Infinite range; triggers when aiming at a target. |
| **E** | Armor Diver | Spammable | Rapid weapon summons; can be chained repeatedly. |
| **T** | Gate Burst | AoE | Tapping summons weapons around target; holding summons around self. |
| **Y** | Enkidu Chain | Counter/Stun | If aimed: Stuns. If not aimed: Functions as a counter. |
| **Z** | Chain of Fate | Utility | Drags the opponent toward Gilgamesh for combos. |
| **G** | Enuma Elish | Ultimate | Massive AoE that sucks in players and deals fatal damage. |

### Advanced Combat Mechanics
Gilgamesh thrives on maintaining pressure. The **E skill (Armor Diver)** is particularly potent because it lacks a significant cooldown, allowing you to "infinite combo" players who fail to use their evasive maneuvers.

<Callout type="info" title="Skill Nuance">
The **Y skill** is contextual. In the heat of battle, always try to aim it directly at the enemy to secure the stun, as the counter window is relatively tight.
</Callout>
