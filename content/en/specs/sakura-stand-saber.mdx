<Section icon={Target} title="How to Obtain Sakura Stand Saber Specs">

Securing a **sakura stand saber** spec is a rite of passage for many players. The process is not as simple as using a Stand Arrow; it involves navigating specific NPC quests and collecting rare items that typically drop from high-level encounters or seasonal events. According to the official Sakura Stand Trello, progression usually requires a base "Standless" state and a high Mastery level to ensure the player can handle the spec's power.

The most common path involves the **Holy Grail** item, which acts as the catalyst for the Saber transformation. Players must often prove their worth through combat trials or by accumulating specific currencies like Tokens and Cash, which can be boosted using active game codes.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Required Mastery">
    Ensure your character has reached at least Mastery level 1. Many advanced specs, including Saber, are locked behind mastery gates to prevent low-level players from skipping core progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Standless">
    Use a **Rokakaka Fruit** to reset your current ability. Saber is a "Spec," meaning it is applied directly to your character's base form rather than acting as a summoned Stand.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Quest NPC">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Obtain the Catalyst">
    Acquire the **Holy Grail** or the **Corrupted Grail** for Alter. These items are rare drops or rewards from the questline. Using the item while Standless will grant you the Saber spec.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Move Sets">

The true strength of the **sakura stand saber** lies in its versatile move set. Mastery of these skills allows players to chain long combos that are difficult for opponents to interrupt. Both Artoria and Alter share a similar foundation but differ in the execution of their ultimate abilities and passive buffs.

For Artoria, "Invisible Air" provides a subtle advantage by making it harder for opponents to judge the reach of her blade. In contrast, Saber Alter utilizes "Mana Burst" to significantly increase the damage of her next few attacks, making her one of the most dangerous burst-damage dealers in the game.

### Saber Artoria Move Set
- **M1 Combo**: A series of fast slashes ending in a knockback.
- **Invisible Air (Skill 1)**: A wind-based blast that stuns enemies and increases sword range.
- **Strike Air (Skill 2)**: A forward dash that pierces through blocks.
- **Excalibur (Ultimate)**: A massive beam of light that deals heavy multi-hit damage in a line.

### Saber Alter Move Set
- **M1 Combo**: Heavier, slower hits with higher individual damage.
- **Vortigern (Skill 1)**: A dark energy slash that pulls enemies closer.
- **Dark Mana Burst (Skill 2)**: An explosion of dark energy that provides a temporary damage buff.
- **Excalibur Morgan (Ultimate)**: A wide-range dark beam that applies a "Corruption" debuff, draining enemy health over time.

<Callout type="success" title="Meta Strategy">
In 2026, the most effective combo for Saber is **Skill 1 -> M1 x3 -> Skill 2 -> Ultimate**. This sequence maximizes stun time and ensures the Ultimate hits while the opponent's escape options are on cooldown.
</Callout>

| **Skill 1** | Stun / Range | Pull / Crowd Control |
