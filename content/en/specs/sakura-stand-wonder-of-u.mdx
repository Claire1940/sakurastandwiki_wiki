<Section icon={Target} title="How to Obtain Wonder of U in 2026">

Obtaining the **Sakura Stand Wonder of U** is a multi-step process that separates casual players from dedicated grinders. Unlike basic Stands that only require a Stand Arrow, Wonder of U often requires the user to interact with the game's environmental systems, such as the weather or specific NPC questlines.

The most common path involves the use of the **Mysterious Camera**, an item found in boxes, chests, or through the Bag of Presents. However, simply having the item is not enough; you must use it under the correct conditions to trigger the transformation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire a Base Stand">
    Start by using a Stand Arrow on a Standless character. You are looking for a base that can be evolved or reset easily. Ensure you have a healthy supply of Rokakaka Fruits to reset if you roll unwanted abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm the Mysterious Camera">
    You can obtain this item through item farming routes or by purchasing a Bag of Presents from the Breakthrough Shop. The drop rate is relatively low, so consistency is key.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Wait for Specific Weather">
    Wonder of U and its variations (Summer, Rainy, Winter Time) are often tied to the in-game weather system. Monitor the environment and use the Mysterious Camera when the conditions match the desired form.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete the Evolution">
    Once the conditions are met, use the item while Standless or with the required base. This will consume the item and grant you the Wonder of U ability.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch the Clock">
Some evolutions, like Made in Heaven or certain Wonder of U variants, may require real-life timing (such as a New Moon). Always check the official Trello or Discord announcements before consuming rare items to ensure the conditions are active.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Strategies and Move Breakdown">

The combat loop for **Sakura Stand Wonder of U** revolves around "The Flow of Calamity." Unlike Sukuna or Toji Fushiguro, who want to get in the opponent's face, Wonder of U players should play a "cat and mouse" game. By forcing the opponent to pursue you, you trigger passive damage effects that bypass traditional blocks.

| Move Type | Name | Effect | Strategic Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Passive** | Calamity | Damage on pursuit | Primary damage source |
| **Melee** | Cane Strike | Moderate knockback | Create distance |
| **Ranged** | Debris Fall | AOE Stun | Interrupt enemy combos |
| **Ultimate** | Endless Calamity | Massive environmental damage | Finisher move |

When facing high-mobility Specs like Sandevistan or Naoya Zen'in, Wonder of U users must be patient. Naoya's technical rushdown can be overwhelming, but his reliance on movement makes him highly susceptible to Calamity triggers. Use your AOE stuns to break their momentum and reset the distance.

<Callout type="tip" title="Countering the Meta">
Against Sukuna users, watch out for their Domain Expansion. Your best bet is to use your invincibility frames or distance-closing moves to exit the area before the heavy damage ticks begin.
</Callout>
