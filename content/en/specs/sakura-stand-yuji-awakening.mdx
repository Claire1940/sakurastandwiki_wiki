<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Awakening Quest Guide">

To begin the **sakura stand yuji awakening**, you must seek out the Kusakabe NPC. He is located directly next to Auddy's Shop. Equip your Yuji Itadori spec and speak with him to accept the quest titled **\"The Curse Within\"**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Base Yuji Itadori">
    Reach Mastery 1 to claim the base spec. Equip it before proceeding to the next step.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Kusakabe">
    Travel to Auddy's Shop and find Kusakabe standing nearby. Speak to him to receive \"The Curse Within\" quest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat the Special Grade Curse">
    Locate and defeat exactly 1 Special Grade Curse using your Yuji moveset.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat Fushiguro Toji">
    Track down and defeat Fushiguro Toji. Use your hyperarmor moves to bypass his high-speed physical strikes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Farm Minor Curses & Damage">
    Defeat 10 standard Curses, execute 5 successful parries, and accumulate 3,250 total damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Return to Kusakabe">
    Speak to Kusakabe once all objectives are marked complete to finalize the Shinjuku evolution.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Evolution Complete">
Once you hand in the completed quest, your Yuji Itadori spec permanently evolves into Shinjuku Itadori, unlocking new poses, music tracks, and enhanced skill scaling.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="Quest Requirements and Enemy Locations">

Fulfilling the requirements for \"The Curse Within\" requires visiting specific farming locations and battling tough bosses. Below is the breakdown of the exact objectives needed for the awakening.

| Quest Objective | Target Count | Best Farming Method / Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Kill Special Grade Curses** | 1 | Spawn in designated curse zones; team up if undergeared |
| **Kill Fushiguro Toji** | 1 | Boss spawn arena; utilize parries to create openings |
| **Kill Standard Curses** | 10 | Low-level curse spawn fields; quick to clear with Barrage (E) |
| **Perform Parries** | 5 | Bait slow attacks from basic training dummies or low-tier curses |
| **Deal Overall Damage** | 3,250 | Accumulated naturally while fighting bosses and standard curses |
