  <Step num="4" title="The Mirror and the Depths">

The skills associated with this spec provide a unique tactical advantage in both PvP and PvE scenarios. Unlike high-burst specs like Sukuna or Gojo, the Pilgrim Knight focuses on attrition—wearing down the opponent while maintaining a superior defensive posture.
