Midknight and its evolved form play like pressure specs. You want to start the fight, keep the target stuck, and then cash out before they reset. The kit has enough reach to force movement, and enough stun to make clean confirms feel rewarding.

<Callout type="info" title="PvP Note">
The kit gets stronger when you play with confidence. Use your control tools first, then save the low-health finisher for a real conversion instead of a random swing.
</Callout>
