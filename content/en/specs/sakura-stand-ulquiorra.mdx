<Section icon={Shield} title="Quest Requirements and Target Order">

Ulquiorra’s Isolation quest asks for a broad Hollow clear, including **one of every Hollow type**, plus **Demi-Hollow**, **Fishbone**, **Gillian/Menos Grande**, and **Adjucha**. The best way to handle that list is to group enemies by availability and finish the easiest counts first.

This is not a single-target boss fight. It is a cleanup route. The more you spread your attention, the more time you lose.
