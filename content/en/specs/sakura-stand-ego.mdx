<Section icon={Swords} title="The Red Mist E.G.O. Progression Route">

The Red Mist is widely considered one of the most powerful melee specs in **sakura stand ego** history. It focuses on the "E.G.O. Manifestation" mechanic, granting the player massive lifesteal and high-damage slashes. Unlike banner specs, this requires a dedicated boss-hunting cycle.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Carmen and Q3">
    Navigate to the Dark Alley area. You must interact with the NPC Carmen to begin the lore thread, then find Q3, who serves as the gatekeeper for the Red Mist encounter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Map Enemies">
    You cannot simply walk into the boss arena. You must build progress by defeating standard map enemies. Once your progress is sufficient, talk to Q3 to open the portal to the Red Mist encounter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat The Red Mist Boss">
    This is a multi-phase fight. The boss uses Greater Split: Horizontal and Vertical, which can one-shot unprepared players. Focus on dodging the red flash indicators.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Use Mimicry on Standless">
    Once the boss is defeated, there is a chance to drop the **Mimicry** item. Reset your current ability using a Rokakaka Fruit to become Standless, then use Mimicry to obtain the Spec.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Boss Warning">
The Red Mist boss has a lifesteal mechanic. If you take too many hits, she will heal back to full health, making the fight last indefinitely. Use high-stun abilities to interrupt her combos.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Jinpachi Ego and the Blue Lock Route">

The third pillar of the **sakura stand ego** keyword is the Jinpachi Ego NPC. Located at the Basketball Court, he is the starting point for the Yoichi Isagi spec. This route is unique because it focuses on "Flow" and "Metavision" mechanics rather than traditional anime magic or Stands.

2. **Complete the Trial**: You will be tasked with specific combat and movement challenges.
