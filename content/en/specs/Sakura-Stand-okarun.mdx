<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Obtainment Guide">

Unlocking the **Sakura Stand Okarun** spec requires following a specific quest line initiated during the Winter event. You cannot bypass these steps, and failing the final challenge may force you to restart certain portions of the grind.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect 100 Souls">
    Your first objective is to gather 100 Souls. These items drop randomly from defeating any NPC or Curse on the map. This is widely considered the most tedious part of the quest, often taking players 1-2 hours of active grinding.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Turbo Baba">
    Once you have all 100 Souls in your inventory, find the Turbo Baba NPC. She is typically found in secluded areas of the map during the event period. Handing over the souls will progress the dialogue to the next phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Find Momo">
    After speaking with Turbo Baba, you will be tasked with finding Momo. This interaction triggers the final and most difficult challenge: the Escape from Granny mini-game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Escape from Granny">
    You will be transported to a unique obstacle course. You must survive the chase by dodging rocks and barriers. If you hit too many obstacles, Granny will catch you, and you may lose progress.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Mastering the Granny Escape Mini-Game">

The "Escape from Granny" segment is the mechanical gatekeeper for the **Sakura Stand Okarun** spec. Unlike the rest of the game, this part plays like a third-person runner or "Obby." The camera shakes significantly to simulate the intensity of the chase, making it difficult to spot obstacles far in advance.

To succeed, you must focus on the center of the screen and react quickly to the rocks appearing in your path. The mini-game doesn't require you to press a "run" button; your character moves forward automatically, and your primary job is to swerve left and right.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Okarun Move Set and Combat Analysis">

Once you obtain the **Sakura Stand Okarun** spec, you gain access to two distinct forms: the Base Form and the Turbo Form. The Base Form is relatively limited, primarily utilizing basic M1 combos. However, using the "Huge" skill allows you to transform, unlocking the true potential of the spec.

| Key | Skill Name | Description | Combat Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **R** | Drop Kick | A lunging kick that knocks back foes | Gap closer or finisher |
