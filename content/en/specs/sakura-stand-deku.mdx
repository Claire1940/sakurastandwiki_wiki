<Section icon={Shield} title="Prerequisites and Initial Requirements">

Before you can even speak to the first NPC in the **sakura stand deku** quest line, your character must meet specific account-wide criteria. Sakura Stand uses a Mastery system to gate late-game content, and Deku is firmly positioned as a mid-to-late game Spec.

The most important factor is your current ability state. You cannot progress through the One For All stages if you have another Stand or Spec equipped. You must use a **Rokakaka Fruit** to return to the **Standless** state before interacting with Kuzma.

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Requirement">
Attempting to talk to quest NPCs while holding a Stand will result in them ignoring you. Always double-check your Standless status and Mastery level in the main menu.
</Callout>

<Section icon={HelpCircle} title="Step-by-Step Acquisition Guide">

The process of unlocking the **sakura stand deku** Spec is divided into three distinct phases: the Awakening, the Strengthening, and the Final Inheritance. Follow these steps precisely to avoid wasting time on incorrect NPCs.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Find Kuzma and Gain OFA">
    First, find **Kuzma**. He spawns in various locations but is most commonly found near the outskirts of the main town or near the forest entrance. Talk to him while Standless (and at Mastery 3) to receive the base **One For All** ability. This is Stage 1.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Progress Through OFA Stages">
    To move from Stage 1 to higher percentages of power, you must complete combat tasks. This usually involves dealing damage or defeating a set number of NPCs while using the OFA base moveset. Continue this until you reach the requirement for the boss phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat the Deku Boss">
    Travel to the **Ruined City**. Here, you must find and defeat the **Deku Boss**. He uses high-speed dashes and explosive "Delaware Smash" attacks. Defeating him is the only way to obtain **One For All's Grace**, a critical progression item.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The NameClan Quest">
    With One For All's Grace in your inventory, head to the **Forest Tent area** and locate the NPC **NameClan**. He will give you a final Standless-only quest. This typically involves a high damage-taken or damage-dealt requirement without the help of a Stand.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Consume All Might's Hair">
    Upon completing NameClan's task, you will receive **All Might's Hair**. Use this item while in your final OFA stage to permanently unlock the full **Deku Spec**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Evolution Complete">
Once you consume the hair, your moveset will change to the full Deku Spec, featuring improved animations, higher damage scaling, and the "Shoot Style" passive.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Deku Boss Fight Strategy">

The Deku boss in the Ruined City is the primary roadblock for most players seeking the **sakura stand deku** Spec. He is fast, aggressive, and can easily combo a player from full health to zero if they are caught without a dodge.

**Video Highlights:**
- **Phase 1**: Boss uses standard smashes and predictable lunges.
- **Phase 2**: Boss enters "Full Cowl," increasing speed and adding shockwaves to attacks.
- **Counter-play**: Focus on parrying the initial dash to open a window for a counter-attack.

<Callout type="info" title="Combat Mechanics">
The boss has a specific "Stun Bar." Using heavy attacks or parries will deplete this bar. Once it breaks, the boss is immobile for 3 seconds, allowing you to unleash your highest-damage combo.
</Callout>

| Boss Attack | Effect | How to Avoid |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Detroit Smash** | Massive AOE damage | Jump or dash backward when he winds up |
| **Delaware Smash** | Ranged air pressure | Side-step the projectile |
| **St. Louis Smash** | High-speed kick | Perfect block or parry the startup |
| **Manchester Smash** | Overhead guard break | Do not block; dash out of the impact zone |

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Moveset and Combat Tier Analysis">

The **sakura stand deku** Spec is widely considered an **S-Tier** combat choice in the 2026 meta. Its ability to bridge gaps instantly and break guards makes it a nightmare for Stand users who rely on defensive zoning.

The Spec utilizes a "Percentage System." As you fight, you can toggle between different power outputs (e.g., 5%, 20%, 100%). Higher percentages increase your damage but drain your stamina or health over time, requiring careful management.

| Move Name | Keybind | Description | Combat Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shoot Style** | Passive | Increases movement speed and kick damage | Mobility |
| **Vanish Smash** | E | A teleport-style strike behind the enemy | Opener |
| **Iron Sole Strike** | R | A heavy kick that breaks blocks | Guard Break |
| **100% Smash** | T | A massive cinematic attack with huge knockback | Finisher |
| **Blackwhip** | F | Pulls enemies toward you or pulls you to terrain | Utility |

<Callout type="tip" title="Combo Suggestion">
Start with **Blackwhip (F)** to pull the enemy in, immediately follow with **Iron Sole Strike (R)** to break their guard, and finish with a **Vanish Smash (E)** combo. This sequence is difficult to escape without a burst or escape move.
</Callout>
