<Section icon={Map} title="How to Obtain the Vergil Spec">

Getting the Vergil Spec is a multi-step journey that requires patience and a bit of luck. The core of this Spec is the **Yamato**, a weapon that cannot be pulled from a standard Stand Arrow. You must interact with the world and potentially face off against challenging odds to claim it.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Mastery 1 with Standless">
    Before you can even think about wielding the Yamato, you must prove your basic combat prowess. Use the **Standless** base state to farm NPCs or complete basic quests until you hit Mastery 1. This unlocks the ability to interact with higher-tier Spec items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Obtain the Yamato Item">
    The Yamato is the catalyst for the Vergil Spec. It is commonly found as a **rare drop from Chests** or can be obtained through specific **Event Banners** like the Arrow of Novae. Some seasonal updates also place the Yamato in the **Breakthrough Shop** for a limited time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip Standless and Use Yamato">
    Once the Yamato is in your inventory, ensure you have used a **Rokakaka Fruit** to reset your current ability. With the Standless Spec active, select the Yamato and use it. Your character will undergo a transformation animation, and the Vergil moveset will be bound to your keys.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Requirement">
Do not use the Yamato while you have a Stand or another Spec active. You **must be Standless** to successfully transition into the Vergil Spec. Using it while another ability is active may result in the item being consumed without giving you the Spec.
</Callout>

| Requirement | Details | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Base State** | Standless | Mandatory |
| **Mastery Level** | Level 1+ Recommended | High |
| **Core Item** | **Yamato** | Mandatory |
| **Reset Item** | Rokakaka Fruit | High |

</Section>

<Section icon={Zap} title="Vergil Moveset and Combo Analysis">

The Vergil Spec is defined by its "Concentration" mechanic. The more you land hits without taking damage, the more powerful your moves become. In Sakura Stand, this translates to increased range on your Judgement Cuts and faster startup frames for your ultimate abilities.

**Detailed Moveset Breakdown:**

- **[LMB] - Yamato Combo**: A series of fast slashes that deal consistent damage and build Concentration.
- **[Q] - Air Trick**: Instantly teleports the player to the nearest target. This is your primary gap-closer and combo extender.
- **[E] - Judgement Cut**: Vergil unsheathes and sheathes his sword so fast it creates a distortion in space. This deals multi-hit damage in a medium radius.
- **[R] - Rapid Slash**: A forward dash that slices through all enemies in a line. Perfect for escaping corners or initiating a fight.
- **[F] - Judgement Cut End**: The ultimate move. Vergil freezes time briefly and covers the entire screen in a massive Judgement Cut. 

<Callout type="info" title="Combat Mechanic: Mirage Blades">
By pressing **[V]**, you can summon Mirage Blades that hover around you. These can be fired manually or set to automatically track enemies, providing constant chip damage while you focus on your main sword combos.
</Callout>

| **Rapid Slash** | Linear Pierce | Long | 8 Seconds |
