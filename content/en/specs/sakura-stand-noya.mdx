<Section icon={Target} title="How to Obtain Naoya Zen'in">

Obtaining the **sakura stand noya** Spec requires a specific sequence of NPC interactions and combat challenges. Unlike Stands that can be rolled from an Arrow, Naoya is a "Spec," meaning it is tied to your character's progression and quest completion. You must be prepared to travel to specific locations like the Abandoned Shrine and face unique encounters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Preparation and Requirements">
    Ensure your character is **Standless** (use a Rokakaka Fruit if necessary) and that you have reached at least **Mastery 1**. Crucially, you must not have any other Jujutsu Kaisen Spec (like Itadori or Gojo) currently equipped, or the quest NPC will not trigger the correct dialogue.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Naobito Zen'in">
    Travel to the **Abandoned Shrine**. Here, you will find the NPC **Naobito**. Talk to him to begin the Zen'in clan progression route. He will task you with proving your worth as a member of the clan, which involves a series of combat trials against map enemies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Zen'in Progression Route">
    Follow the quest markers provided by Naobito. You will likely encounter **John Zen'in** as part of the encounter pool. This phase requires consistent farming of specific cursed-themed enemies to build your reputation within the clan.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Naoya Raid">
    Once the preliminary steps are complete, you must enter the **Naoya Raid**. This is a challenging combat instance where you must prove your technical skill. Clearing this raid is the final hurdle before unlocking the Spec.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Final Unlock">
    After successfully completing the raid and meeting all dialogue requirements, you will be granted the Naoya Zen'in Spec. Your character will now have access to Projection Sorcery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Quest Softlocks">
Players have reported softlocks if they switch Specs midway through the Zen'in route. It is highly recommended to stay Standless throughout the entire process until the Naoya Spec is officially added to your inventory.
</Callout>

</Section>
