<Section icon={Map} title="How to Summon Dio Brando">

Summoning the boss requires navigating to the deepest parts of the map and defeating another powerful opponent first. Players must secure Kujo's Hat, a rare drop from Jotaro Kujo, who resides in the depths. 

To find Jotaro Kujo, enter the cave system known as the depths. Once inside, immediately head to the left path to locate Jotaro's private chamber. Defeating Jotaro gives you a chance to obtain Kujo's Hat. Once you have the hat, you must equip the stand "The World" to sacrifice the hat and initiate the battle with the boss.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Navigate to the Depths">
    Locate the main cave entrance on the map and head inside. Follow the path leading downward into the depths.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Jotaro's Chamber">
    After entering the cave system, take an immediate left. Follow the corridor until you reach the chamber where Jotaro Kujo spawns.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm Kujo's Hat">
    Engage Jotaro Kujo in combat. Defeat him repeatedly until he drops the rare accessory item known as Kujo's Hat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Perform the Sacrifice">
    Equip the stand "The World". Use Kujo's Hat at the summoning altar in the depths to sacrifice the item and spawn the boss.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Step | Objective | Location | Key Requirement |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Locate Jotaro | Left side of Depths cave | None |
| 2 | Obtain Hat | Jotaro's Chamber | Defeat Jotaro Kujo |
| 3 | Summon Boss | Summon Altar | Use The World Stand |

<Callout type="warning" title="Summoning Warning">
Do not attempt to sacrifice Kujo's Hat without having The World active. Doing so will waste the item without spawning the boss, forcing you to farm Jotaro Kujo all over again.
</Callout>
