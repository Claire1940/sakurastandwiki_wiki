<Section icon={Map} title="Overview of the Made in Heaven Evolution Line">

In the current 2026 meta of Sakura Stand, **Made in Heaven** remains one of the most sought-after Stands due to its lore significance and devastating speed-based kit. Inspired by JoJo's Bizarre Adventure Part 6: Stone Ocean, this Stand represents the pinnacle of the Pucci evolution line. Unlike basic Stands obtained through a simple Stand Arrow, Made in Heaven requires a multi-stage progression that tests a player's patience, farming efficiency, and understanding of game-world triggers.

The journey begins with **White Snake**, a Stand that provides a solid foundation but lacks the raw power of endgame Specs. From there, players must navigate the "Green Baby" ritual to achieve **C-Moon**, a Stand that manipulates gravity and serves as the immediate precursor to Made in Heaven. The final transition is arguably the most atmospheric challenge in the game, involving environmental navigation and real-world time constraints.

<Callout type="tip" title="Mastery Check">
Before starting this route, ensure you are comfortable with the C-Moon moveset. You will need to defend yourself during the "Highest Peak" journey, and many players fail the evolution because they are unprepared for high-level NPC or player interference.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Obtain Made in Heaven: Step-by-Step">

Unlocking **sakura stand made in heaven** is a test of precision. You cannot simply buy your way to this power; you must earn it through a series of specific interactions within the Roblox experience. The process is divided into three major phases: the initial pull, the first evolution, and the final ascension.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain White Snake">
    The foundation of this route is White Snake. You must use a **Stand Arrow** while in the **Standless** state. Since the Arrow pool contains multiple Stands like Star Platinum and Crazy Diamond, you may need to use **Rokakaka Fruits** to reset your ability until White Snake is successfully rolled.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Evolve into C-Moon">
    Once you have White Snake, you must obtain the **Green Baby**. This item is typically found through rare drops or specific quest rewards. Using the Green Baby on your White Snake will transform it into C-Moon, granting you gravity-based abilities that are essential for the next phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Acquire Dio's Diary">
    The most critical item for the final step is **Dio's Diary**. This is a high-rarity item that can be found in chests or dropped by specific bosses. Do not attempt to climb the Highest Peak without this item in your inventory, as the ritual will not trigger.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The New Moon Ritual">
    With C-Moon equipped and Dio's Diary in your inventory, you must travel to the **Highest Peak** location. The final evolution only occurs during a **real-life New Moon**. Check the in-game moon cycle or community Discord trackers to ensure the timing is correct.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="New Moon Timing">
The "New Moon" requirement refers to the actual moon phase tracked by the game server. If you reach the peak and use the diary during any other phase, the evolution will fail, and you may lose your progress or items depending on the current patch mechanics.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Made in Heaven Move Set and Combat Mechanics">

Once the evolution is complete, the player gains access to a kit that revolves around **Time Acceleration**. Made in Heaven is designed for players who prefer a "hit-and-run" or "high-pressure" playstyle. Its base movement speed is significantly higher than most other Stands in the game, allowing you to dictate the pace of any encounter.

The core of the Made in Heaven experience is the ability to speed up your own animations while slowing down the perception of your opponents. In PvP, this makes you incredibly difficult to track, as your dash distance and attack frequency far exceed standard combat limits.

| Move Name | Keybind | Description | Combat Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Infinite Speed** | E | A rapid-fire barrage of punches. | Core DPS / Combo Starter |
| **Knives of Heaven** | R | Throws multiple knives at the target. | Ranged Pressure |
| **Time Acceleration** | H | Increases movement and attack speed. | Buff / Meta Defining |
| **Universe Reset** | J | Large AoE that deals massive damage. | Ultimate / Finisher |
| **Dimension Slice** | T | A fast forward-moving slash. | Mobility / Gap Closer |

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Strategy">
Always activate **Time Acceleration** before engaging in a full combo. The speed boost reduces the wind-up time of your "Infinite Speed" barrage, making it nearly impossible for opponents to Perfect Block if timed correctly.
</Callout>

To reach the pinnacle of the Pucci line, you must be efficient with your resource gathering. The items required for Made in Heaven are not common, and farming them requires a strategic approach to the different zones in Sakura Stand.

| Item Name | Drop Location | Farming Method | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stand Arrow** | Starter Town / Chests | Open low-tier boxes or farm NPCs. | High (Starter) |

Farming **Dio's Diary** is often the biggest bottleneck. We recommend focusing on elite NPC spawns near the mountain areas. These enemies have a higher drop rate for "Lore Items" compared to the standard thugs found in the city. Additionally, keeping a stock of **Tokens** is useful for trading with other players in the Discord community if the RNG is not in your favor.
