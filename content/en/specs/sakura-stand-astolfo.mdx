<Section icon={Swords} title="Astolfo (Saber) Moveset & Mechanics">

The **sakura stand astolfo** spec features a highly aggressive, sword-based moveset that excels at close-to-mid-range combat. Its primary strength lies in its unique passive ability, which turns standard encounters into high-damage critical bursts.

### Passive Ability: Critical Hits
Most attacks executed with this spec have a baseline **25% chance to trigger a Critical Hit**, which amplifies damage output. Your Crit. Rate and Crit. Damage scale directly with your **Damage Income Multiplier** (any value above 1.0 increases your critical statistics). Players often equip the Starry Phantom title to optimize this passive.

| Keybind | Move Name | Description | Cooldown |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **M1** | Oath Of Saber | A 5-strike rapid combo with a segmented saber, ending in circular slashes | None |
| **E** | Dance Of The Knight | A spinning AoE slash that creates a localized tornado, damaging nearby targets | Short |
| **R** | Wheel Of Fate | A rolling advance followed by two consecutive heavy downward slashes | Medium |
| **T** | Followup Carnage | A forward dash that transitions into a swift upward and downward slash | Medium |
| **Y** | Imperial Chain | A teleporting blitz that slices the target multiple times before a final thrust | Medium |
| **G** | Vulcano Caligarente | A cinematic Noble Phantasm that launches, wraps, and slashes the target | Long |
| **H** | Warrior's Saber | A highly destructive, steerable magenta flame beam that explodes at the end | Long |
| **Q** | Majestic Triumphal Return | A self-buff creating an aura that boosts damage, crit damage, and crit rate | 30s Duration |

<Callout type="tip" title="Combos and Buffs">
Always activate **Majestic Triumphal Return (Q)** before initiating a full combo. The 25% damage boost combined with increased critical rates makes moves like Imperial Chain (Y) and Warrior's Saber (H) absolutely devastating.
</Callout>
