Unlocking the **sakura stand sancho** Spec requires a blend of exploration and resource management. You cannot simply roll for this Spec using a **Stand Arrow**. Instead, you must follow a dedicated questline that involves the **Sakura Society's** latest NPC additions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Mastery 1">
    Before you can interact with the quest-giving NPCs, you must reach **Mastery 1**. This is done by using any basic Stand or Spec (like **Standless** or **Hamon**) and defeating NPCs. Premium users receive a **1.15x XP gain**, which significantly speeds up this process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Reset to Standless">
    Visit **Auddy's Shop** or find a **Rokakaka Fruit** in a chest to reset your character. You must be **Standless** to initiate the Sancho dialogue.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Sancho Spirit">
    Travel to the **Abandoned Shrine** area (the same area used for the **Naoya Zen'in** route). Look for a small, flickering blue flame behind the main Torii gate. This is the Sancho Spirit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resource Sacrifice">
    The Spirit will demand **2,500 Cash** and **50 Tokens**. Tokens are best farmed through daily quests or by defeating bosses like **Toji Fushiguro** or the **Red Mist**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Combat Trial">
    You must survive for 60 seconds against a shadow version of yourself. Do not focus on killing it; focus on dodging. If you take less than 100 damage, the Spec is granted immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success!">
Once the trial is complete, you will automatically equip the Sancho Spec. Your UI will update to show the new "Wind-Up" gauge.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Sancho Moveset and Combat Mechanics">

The core of the **sakura stand sancho** experience is the **Wind-Up Gauge**. As you land M1 (basic) attacks, the gauge fills, increasing your movement speed and the damage of your special skills. If you are hit, the gauge resets by 25%.

| Move Name | Input | Description | Cooldown |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rapid Thrust** | Q | A forward dash followed by three quick stabs. | 5s |
| **Gale Spin** | E | An AoE spin that knocks back nearby enemies. | 8s |
| **Spirit Leap** | R | A vertical jump that allows for mid-air recovery. | 12s |
| **Final Accord** | T | A cinematic grab that deals massive damage based on Gauge. | 25s |
| **Vanish** | F | A counter-move that teleports you behind the attacker. | 15s |

<Callout type="info" title="Pro Insight">
The **Vanish (F)** move is your most important tool in the 2026 meta. It has a 0.2s startup, making it one of the fastest counters in the game, capable of interrupting **Sukuna's** domain-style pressure if timed perfectly.
</Callout>
