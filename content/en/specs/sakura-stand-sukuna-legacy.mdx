<Section icon={Shield} title="Moveset Breakdown: Mastering the Cursed King">

Once you unlock the **sakura stand sukuna legacy**, understanding the nuances of his moveset is the difference between a mid-tier player and a meta-dominator. Sukuna's kit is built around high-speed slashes and devastating cursed energy bursts. His "Awakening" state is particularly dangerous, as it enhances the range and speed of all Dismantle-based attacks.

**Core Abilities:**
- **Dismantle (E)**: A quick, long-range slash that can be used to poke enemies or finish combos.
- **Cleave (R)**: A powerful melee grab that deals massive damage based on the opponent's current health.
- **Spider Thread (V)**: A mobility tool that allows Sukuna to close gaps or escape dangerous AOE zones.
- **Fire Arrow (F)**: A high-damage projectile with a lingering burn effect, perfect for mid-range pressure.
- **Malevolent Shrine (T)**: The ultimate Domain Expansion. It creates a massive field where all enemies take continuous slashing damage.
