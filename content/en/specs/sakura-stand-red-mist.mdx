<Section icon={Swords} title="The Mimicry and Kali Boss Fight Breakdown">

The battle for the **sakura stand red mist** spec is divided into two distinct encounters. First, you face the Mimicry entity, and subsequently, you must defeat Kali (The Red Mist herself).

**Phase 1: Mimicry**
Mimicry starts with claw-based attacks. Its most dangerous moves are "Hello?" and "GOODBYE." Once it reaches 40% HP, it retreats into an invulnerable egg state. You must deal rapid damage during the "molting" period to prevent it from regaining too much strength. In its second form, it wields a flesh-scythe with increased range and speed.

**Phase 2: Kali (The Red Mist)**
After Mimicry falls, a cutscene triggers Kali's arrival. She uses the full Red Mist moveset. At 50% HP, she manifests her E.G.O, regenerating her health to full and gaining lifesteal. At 10% HP, she will once again regenerate to 50% HP, doubling her lifesteal capabilities.

| Boss Phase | Key Mechanics | Recommended Strategy |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Mimicry (Claws) | Fast M1s, "Hello?" dash | Keep distance, use ranged pokes |
| Mimicry (Scythe) | High bleed, wide arcs | Block the vertical slashes |
| Kali (Base) | Counter-heavy, fast combos | Don't spam; wait for openings |
| Kali (E.G.O) | Teleportation, massive AOE | Stay mobile, avoid "Great Split" |

<Callout type="success" title="Victory Tip">
Using specs with percentage-based damage, such as Toji's "Soul Splitter" (T move), can significantly speed up the fight against Kali's massive health pool.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Navigating the Dark Alley Maze">

To unlock the true potential of the **sakura stand red mist** spec, you must find the secret NPC in the Dark Alleyway. This location is notorious for its confusing layout and the "Distortion" visual effects that clutter your screen.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter the Alley">
    Locate the dark alleyway entrance near the Battlefield area. As you enter, your screen will begin to turn red and display "Distortion" text.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate the Maze">
    Follow this exact path: Take the first **Left**, then a **Right**. Continue until you see another opening, take a **Left**, followed by another **Left**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reach the Open Area">
    Follow the long straight alley until it opens into a large courtyard. The NPC (resembling Carmen) will be waiting there.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Accept the Quest">
    Talk to the NPC to receive the "Manifest E.G.O" quest. This is required to use the Q transformation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Distortion Effects">
The screen will show fake item drops like "+1 Hogyoku" or "-999 Aura." Ignore these; they are purely visual distractions designed to make the maze harder to navigate.
</Callout>

</Section>

To spawn the Black Silence, you typically need the **One For All (OFA)** spec at Stage 4. Farming the Deku boss with OFA increases the chance of the Black Silence appearing. When he spawns, the environment turns into a "Black Star" arena.
