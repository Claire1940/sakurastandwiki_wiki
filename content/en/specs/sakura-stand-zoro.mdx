<Section icon={Swords} title="Zoro Moveset and Combat Strategy">

The **Sakura Stand Zoro** spec is renowned for its aggressive playstyle. It excels in close-quarters combat but features several "Air Slashes" that provide necessary range. Understanding the cooldowns and combo potential of each move is critical for both PVE boss hunting and PVP encounters.

In the current version, Zoro's moveset is divided into light attacks and heavy specialized skills. The **Onigiri** move remains the bread and butter of your combo, providing a dash that can bypass certain blocks. Following this up with **Tatsu Maki** (Dragon Twister) allows you to lift enemies into the air, setting them up for a devastating finisher.

| Skill Key | Move Name | Effect Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **LMB** | Triple Slash | Basic 3-hit combo with high knockback |
| **E** | Onigiri | Forward dash slash, pierces through blocks |
| **R** | Shishi Sonson | High-speed dash; stuns the target on hit |
| **T** | Tatsu Maki | AOE tornado that lifts enemies |
| **Y** | 1080 Pound Ho | Long-range projectile slash |
| **H** | Sanzen Sekai | Ultimate move; massive AOE damage |

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategy Note">
Always initiate with **Shishi Sonson** if the enemy is at a distance. The stun duration is long enough to guarantee a full **Onigiri** into **Tatsu Maki** combo, which can take out nearly 40% of an average player's health bar.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="The Gilgamesh Update: A New Rival Spec">

While many players focus exclusively on **Sakura Stand Zoro**, the recent 2026 update introduced the **Gilgamesh** spec, which shares some similarities in terms of high-tier power but uses a completely different obtainment method. As shown in recent gameplay updates, Gilgamesh is currently a "Banner" unit but can also be earned through a grueling questline.

To obtain Gilgamesh, you must participate in the **Holy Grail** event. This mode is notoriously difficult, featuring a closing "storm" and a mechanic that reduces all players to 1 HP. Winning the raid grants a key, which is the first of two required items.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Win the Holy Grail">
    Enter the Holy Grail raid and survive until the end. Use specs with high I-frames like **Stark** or **Gojo** to survive the final 1 HP phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Complete the Epic Quest">
    Talk to the NPC at the highest peak to start the "Epic of Gilgamesh" quest. This requires killing specific bosses and players.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Kill Dio and Toji">
    Locate and defeat the **Dio** and **Toji** bosses. These can be spawned using specific items like the Vampire Mask or Cursed Tools.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="PVP Requirements">
    Defeat a player using the **Astalo** spec and win at least one 1v1 match in the arena mode.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Final Ritual">
    Use the chat command `/e makingshape` 20 times to finalize the quest and receive the **Epic of Gilgamesh** item.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Warning">
The Holy Grail match has a long cooldown (approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes if you lose). It is highly recommended to team up with friends to ensure a win, as the final phase is extremely chaotic.
</Callout>

</Section>
