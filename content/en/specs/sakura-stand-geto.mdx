<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Quest Walkthrough">

Once you meet the initial conditions, the quest for **sakura stand geto** follows a linear but difficult path. You must interact with the environment and specific NPCs to progress through the stages of Geto's transformation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Become the Sukuna Vessel">
    You cannot simply talk to Geto to start. You must be in a server with at least 5 players. Every 5 minutes, the game rolls a 50% chance to turn one player into a Sukuna Vessel. You will receive a screen notification when you become the Vessel.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Suguru Geto">
    With the Vessel status active, find the Suguru Geto NPC. He will sense the power within you and offer to show you "the way he creates his world." Accept the quest to begin the official objectives.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Sorcerer Hunter Trial">
    You must defeat Toji Fushiguro. You will need tokens to hire/spawn the Sorcerer Hunter. In combat, Toji is fast, but he can occasionally be exploited by trapping him in map geometry or using long-range stuns.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Curse Slaughter">
    Kill 15 regular curses. These spawn naturally around the map. Slaughtering players or other entities often increases the frequency of these spawns. This is the easiest part of the quest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Special Grade Hunt">
    This is the final hurdle. You must wait for a Special Grade Curse to spawn. Because of the 0.5% spawn rate, many players use community Discord servers to find active boss spawns in different instances. Once it spawns, deal enough damage to receive credit for the kill.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: Boss Credit">
When hunting the Special Grade Curse, ensure you are dealing consistent damage. If the boss is "flung" or glitches out of the map (a known issue), you may not receive credit and will have to wait for another spawn.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Strategy and PVP Tips">

Mastering the **sakura stand geto** spec requires a balance between melee pressure and spirit management. While Geto has powerful tools, he can be punished if his summons are timed poorly.

**Key Combat Strategies:**
- **Stun Locking**: Use the Playful Cloud M1s to keep opponents close, then transition into a spirit summon (H) to extend the stun duration.
- **Uzumaki Timing**: Never fire Uzumaki blindly. It has a significant startup animation. Use it when an opponent is stuck in a heavy stun or is recovering from a dash.
- **Resource Management**: Keep an eye on your absorbed curses. If you run out, your spirit summons will be unavailable, leaving you with only basic melee and the pistol.

| Matchup | Strategy | Danger Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Close Range** | Use Playful Cloud M1s and Whirling Cloud | Medium |
| **Long Range** | Pressure with Cursed Pistol and aimed Uzumaki | Low |
| **High Mobility** | Use Cyclops spirit for wider AOE coverage | High |

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Advice">
Geto excels in team fights. His Uzumaki can hit multiple targets, and his spirits provide excellent zoning capabilities. In 1v1 scenarios, focus on landing the Playful Cloud stuns before committing to your high-damage spirit moves.
</Callout>
