<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Obtainment Path">

Unlocking the full potential of the **sakura stand ichigo** spec requires patience, strategy, and a significant amount of grinding. Before you begin your journey, you must prepare your character. First, ensure that you are completely Standless. If you currently have an active stand or spec equipped, you can consume a Rokakaka Fruit to reset your character back to the default state. Rokakaka Fruits can be purchased directly from Auddy's Shop or found in random item chests scattered across the map. Additionally, make sure you have at least $2000 cash in your inventory to cover the entry fee for Hueco Mundo, the dark dimension where Hollows spawn.

The portal to Hueco Mundo is a large, glowing rectangular gateway located directly to the left of the Urahara Shop in Sakurian City. Once you pay the $2000 fee, you can enter the portal as many times as you like during your play session. In Hueco Mundo, you will face various types of Hollows, ranging from basic jumpy enemies to massive, tanky giants. Defeating these enemies is the core loop required to progress through the first two stages of the spec.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlocking Stage 1 (Zanpakuto)">
    Equip Standless and enter Hueco Mundo. Locate and defeat basic Hollows. Each defeated Hollow has a **25% chance** to drop the Stage 1 Zanpakuto directly into your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Grinding Stage 2 (Shikai)">
    Equip your newly acquired Stage 1 spec. You must defeat more Hollows in Hueco Mundo while using this spec. Because Stage 1 only features basic M1 attacks, this step can be slow. Each kill has a **15% chance** to unlock Stage 2.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Completing Kisuke's Quest (Stage 3)">
    Reach Mastery Level 3 and equip Stage 2. Speak to Kisuke near the Urahara Shop and pay $2000 to accept his quest. You must defeat **25 basic Hollows** and **1 Adjuchas** (the elite purple Hollow) to unlock Stage 3.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reaching Stage 4 (Vasto Lorde)">
    To obtain the final form, you must farm Adjuchas (**20% drop rate**) or Menos Grande (**10% drop rate**) in Hueco Mundo to obtain a Hogyoku Fragment. Once obtained, select the fragment from your inventory and use it while equipping Stage 3.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Mugetsu Skill Penalty">
The ultimate move Mugetsu (H key) deals catastrophic damage to enemies but will instantly strip you of the Ichigo spec and revert your character to Standless. To regain the spec, you must die and respawn, which restores your previous stage.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Moveset and Combos Breakdown">

The combat style of the **sakura stand ichigo** spec is highly dynamic, shifting from simple sword strikes in the early stages to devastating area-of-effect energy blasts and defensive counters in Stage 4. In its base form, Ichigo relies on quick, successive slashes that apply bleeding damage to opponents, slowly draining their health over time. The primary offensive tool in this state is Getsuga Slash (Q), which fires three long-distance slashes that can cut off an opponent's arm on the third hit, severely limiting their offensive options.

When players activate Hollow Mask Mode (Y), their combat profile changes dramatically. For 30 seconds, Ichigo gains a massive 50% damage boost, allowing you to melt boss health bars in seconds. However, this power comes at a cost: your defense is reduced by 20%, making you highly vulnerable to counter-attacks. Stage 4 introduces the Vasto Lorde transformation, which replaces the standard moveset with high-damage moves like Crushing Roar (R) and Cero Blast (T), alongside a powerful passive counter that blocks incoming attacks and punishes aggressive opponents.

<Section icon={Target} title="Farming Strategies and Checklist">

Farming the necessary materials and defeating elite enemies in Hueco Mundo can be incredibly challenging, especially on public servers where other players can interfere with your progress. To farm efficiently, it is highly recommended to use a private server. This ensures that you have exclusive access to all Hollow and Adjuchas spawns, allowing you to maximize your token and item gain without the risk of being attacked by hostile players.

When fighting Adjuchas, the key is to maintain your distance and avoid trading blows directly. Adjuchas are incredibly tanky and deal massive damage with their close-range attacks. The most effective strategy is the "M1 and Dash" method. Perform a full four-hit M1 combo to stun the enemy, immediately use your Flash Step (C) to dash backward out of their attack range, and wait for your cooldowns to reset before engaging again. If you are struggling, you can also utilize ranged specs to chip away at their health from a safe distance.
