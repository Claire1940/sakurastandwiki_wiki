<Section icon={Shield} title="Best Combat Patterns for Wild Hunt">

Wild Hunt works best when you make the opponent choose badly. If they block, you threaten block breaks. If they move, the spirit summon and coffin pressure can still force their pathing. If they attack carelessly, the counter becomes your hardest punish.

The cleanest approach is to start with safe pressure, then shift into a bait. Do not force the counter every exchange. Save it for moments when the enemy is likely to swing into you or panic after seeing the purple flash.

**Pattern Table**

| Situation | Best Action | Result |
|---|---|---|
| Enemy blocks often | Use E or LMB pressure | Threatens a break |
| Enemy runs away | Use Y or G | Forces movement |
| Enemy attacks into you | Use H / (H)H | High-reward punish |
| Enemy is cornered | Mix all three | Limits escape options |
