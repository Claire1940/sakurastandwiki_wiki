<Section icon={Map} title="How to Get Kaneki in Sakura Stand">

Obtaining the **Sakura Stand Kaneki** Spec requires a combination of specific item farming and quest completion. Unlike basic Stands that only require a Stand Arrow, Kaneki is part of the "Quest-Heavy Anime Specs" category, similar to the Roland or Sandevistan routes. You must prepare your character by reaching the necessary Mastery level before the NPC will interact with you.

The process begins with the "Standless" state. If you currently have an active Stand or Spec, you must consume a Rokakaka Fruit to reset your abilities. Once you are Standless, you need to locate the Eyepatch Mask, a rare item that drops from specific chests or can be purchased during limited shop rotations at Auddy's Shop.

<Callout type="warning" title="Requirement Check">
Ensure you have at least **Mastery 1** before attempting this route. The quest NPC will ignore players who haven't demonstrated basic combat proficiency.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Preparation and Reset">
    Use a Rokakaka Fruit to return to the Standless state. Ensure your Mastery level is at least 1. Farm for the Eyepatch Mask in the Forest or City areas.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Quest NPC">
    Find the Ghoul Investigator NPC located in the Dark Alley district. Equip the Eyepatch Mask and interact with them to trigger the initiation dialogue.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete the Combat Trials">
    The NPC will task you with defeating 20 map enemies and surviving a mini-boss encounter without using any external healing items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Transformation">
    After completing the trials, return to the NPC. You will be prompted to "Accept the Hunger." Upon confirmation, your character will transform, unlocking the Kaneki Spec.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Kaneki Moveset and Skill Analysis">

The power of **Sakura Stand Kaneki** lies in its versatile moveset. In the 2026 meta, players must master the timing of Kagune releases to maximize damage. Most of Kaneki's skills have low cooldowns but high stamina costs, requiring a balanced approach to combat. The Spec features a mix of broad sweeps for crowd control and focused stabs for single-target elimination.

Kaneki's passive ability, "Ghoul Physiology," provides a constant health regeneration effect that increases as your health drops lower. This makes you exceptionally difficult to kill in prolonged 1v1 scenarios. However, you must stay aggressive; failing to land hits will cause your "Hunger" bar to deplete, temporarily reducing your damage output.

In the current 2026 landscape of Sakura Stand, Kaneki faces stiff competition from Specs like Sukuna, Gojo, and the newly added Naoya Zen'in.
