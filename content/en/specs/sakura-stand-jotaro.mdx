<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Achieve Standless State">
    Ensure your character has no active abilities. If you have a Stand, consume a Rokakaka Fruit. You can farm these from random item spawns or buy them if you have enough Cash.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire Stand Arrows">
    Arrows are common drops from Boxes and Chests scattered across the map. You can also find them in the Arcade or purchase them from specific NPC shops like Auddy's.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Roll for Star Platinum">
    With the Stand Arrow in your inventory, use it while in the Standless state. There is a percentage-based chance to roll Star Platinum among other classic Stands like Crazy Diamond or Golden Experience.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prepare for Evolution">
    Once you have Star Platinum, your next goal is often evolution. For those seeking "The World," you will need to obtain **Unawakened Dio** and eventually **Dio's Bone**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
