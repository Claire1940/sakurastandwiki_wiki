<Section icon={Map} title="How to Unlock sakura stand akaza">

In the intense combat world of Sakura Stand, obtaining the powerful **sakura stand akaza** Spec requires a combination of progression, item farming, and PvP skill. Before you begin this journey, you must obtain and equip the **Lesser Demon** Spec. This serves as the foundation for the entire questline.

Once you have Lesser Demon equipped, you must locate the Muzan NPC, who resides deep inside the Cave. Muzan will act as your quest giver. You will need to complete two consecutive trials to prove your worthiness and transform your Lesser Demon into the formidable Akaza.

| Quest Name | Objective | Target/Requirements | Key Drops Needed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Big Fish Eat Little Fish** | Initial Trial | Talk to Muzan in the Cave | None |
| **Fear the Man Who Has Nothing To Lose** | Final Trial | Kill 4 Initiates & Kokushibo | Compass Needle Pin, Soryu Style Manual |

<Callout type="warning" title="Equip Requirement">
You must keep the **Lesser Demon** Spec equipped during both quests. Defeating Kokushibo or killing players with any other Spec will not count toward your quest progression.
</Callout>

To complete the second quest, "Fear the Man Who Has Nothing To Lose," you must venture into the dangerous **Infinity Castle** instance. Here, you will face regular demon enemies and the final boss, Kokushibo. The items required for the quest have specific drop locations within this zone.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip Lesser Demon & Visit Muzan">
    Locate Muzan inside the Cave. Speak with him while wearing the Lesser Demon Spec to accept the first quest, **Big Fish Eat Little Fish**. Complete this dialogue to unlock the next phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Defeat Demon Slayer Initiates">
    Engage in PvP combat and defeat 4 players who are using the **Demon Slayer Initiate** Spec. You must land the final blow while using Lesser Demon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Conquer the Infinity Castle">
    Enter the Infinity Castle instance. Defeat the standard demon mobs inside to farm the **Soryu Style Manual**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat Kokushibo">
    Reach the end of the Infinity Castle and defeat the boss, **Kokushibo**, using your Lesser Demon Spec. Upon his defeat, obtain the **Compass Needle Pin**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Return to Muzan">
    Bring both the Soryu Style Manual and the Compass Needle Pin back to Muzan in the Cave. Complete the dialogue to receive the Akaza Spec.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Base Moveset and Passive Abilities">

The **sakura stand akaza** Spec offers an aggressive, close-quarters combat style. Before activating your core empowerment stance, you must master the base moveset. Akaza possesses a passive health regeneration feature that helps sustain him during long skirmishes.

Akaza regenerates **5 HP every 5 seconds** while out of combat. If you are actively engaged in combat, this regeneration rate drops to **1 HP every 5 seconds**. This ensures you can recover quickly between skirmishes without relying on external healing items.

| Move Name | Keybind | Base Damage | Description & Combat Effects |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Soryu Strike** | LMB | 25 | A standard 4-hit punch combo dealing 6, 6, 6, and 7 damage. |
| **Leg Type: Explosive Flurry** | E | 24.83 | A rapid succession of kicks that pushes opponents back. |
| **Leg Type: Crown Splitter** | R | 25 | An overhead kick that inflicts continuous bleed damage. |
| **Air Type** | T | 25 | A shockwave strike that projects forward to hit mid-range targets. |
| **Leg Type: Flying Planet Thousand Wheels** | Y | 24 | An upward spiral kick useful for catching airborne enemies. |
| **Annihilation Type** | G | 30 | A heavy forward dash followed by a powerful straight punch. |
| **Disorder** | H | 45 | A chaotic flurry of strikes dealing high burst damage. |
| **Compass Needle** | Q | 2 | Activates the core stance, creating an AOE field. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Combos with Bleed">
Always try to land **Leg Type: Crown Splitter (R)** early in your attack sequence. The bleed damage pressure forces opponents to panic, making them easier to catch with **Disorder (H)**.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Compass Needle Mode Enhancements">

Pressing the **Q key** activates the **Compass Needle** stance. This move deals a minor 2 damage to nearby enemies upon activation and deploys a large area-of-effect (AOE) ring on the ground. While inside this zone or under the influence of the Compass, your moveset undergoes a massive transformation.

The Compass Needle alters the damage values and properties of almost all your offensive skills. It turns Akaza from a standard brawler into a high-tier threat capable of erasing health bars in seconds.

| Enhanced Move Name | Keybind | Buffed Damage | Stance Modifications & Utility |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Leg Type: Explosive Flurry** | E | 34.36 | Increased strike speed and higher overall damage output. |
| **Destructive: Crown Splitter** | R | 30.00 | Enhanced impact radius and increased bleed duration. |
| **Destructive Death: Air Type** | T | 75.00 | Massive ranged shockwave that deals triple the base damage. |
| **Annihilation Type: Punish** | G | 30.00 | Adds block-breaking properties to the forward dash punch. |
| **Final Form: Blue Silver Chaotic Afterglow** | H | 30 - 176 | High-damage ultimate move; requires user to be below 50 HP. |

<Callout type="success" title="Ultimate Stance Requirement">
The **Final Form: Blue Silver Chaotic Afterglow (H)** is your ultimate comeback mechanic. It can deal up to 176 damage depending on your positioning and timing, but it remains locked unless your current HP is **less than 50**.
</Callout>

</Section>
