<Section icon={Swords} title="The Sandevistan Quest Steps">

Once you have all four parts in your inventory, speak to the designated NPC to exchange them for the official quest. This quest is unique because it forces you to play **Standless**, testing your fundamental combat skills without the crutch of a powerful Stand. This phase is designed to prove you are worthy of the cybernetic enhancements.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Engagement">
    Speak to the Cyberpunk NPC with all four parts. Confirm you want to start the quest, which will consume the items permanently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Combat Proficiency">
    Deal a massive amount of damage while Standless. This is best done against high-health NPCs or during boss fights where you can safely land hits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Durability Testing">
    Take and block a specific amount of damage. You can complete this efficiently by letting minor NPCs hit you while you maintain a block or use health-regen items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Trial">
    Win a 1v1 match in the arena while Standless. This is the hardest step, as you will likely face players with fully evolved Stands or Specs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Quest Objective | Requirement | Best Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Deal Damage** | High Threshold | Farm Jotaro or Thugs |
| **Take Damage** | Moderate Threshold | Tank minor NPC hits |
| **Block Damage** | Moderate Threshold | Hold block against fast-hitting NPCs |
| **1v1 Win** | 1 Victory | Use movement to bait out Stand skills |

<Callout type="success" title="1v1 Strategy">
To win the 1v1 Standless, focus on "M1 trades." Most players rely heavily on their Stand skills; if you can dodge their primary attacks, you can win via attrition and superior movement.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Moveset and Skill Analysis">

The **Sakura Stand sandevistan** spec is built for speed, evasion, and sudden bursts of aggression. While the visual model for the arm may appear unfinished in some versions, the mechanical performance is top-tier for PvP enthusiasts who enjoy a "hit-and-run" playstyle.

| Skill Key | Move Name | Description | Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **M1** | Basic Combo | Rapid physical strikes | Combo filler |
| **Q** | Rapid Fire | A flurry of high-speed punches | Damage burst |
| **E** | Face Breaker | A leaping strike with decent range | Gap closer |
| **R** | Rocket Leap | High-mobility jump/flight | Escape/Initiation |
| **H** | Sandevistan | Activates time-dilation effect | God-tier buff |

The "H" key is the defining feature of this spec. When activated, your character enters a hyper-accelerated state, making you significantly harder to hit while allowing you to reposition behind enemies instantly. This is often used to set up a "Face Breaker" or to escape a lethal combo.

<Callout type="info" title="Skill Synergy">
Combine the **H-key activation** with **Face Breaker** to land an unblockable-feeling initiation. The speed boost makes the leap almost instantaneous from the opponent's perspective.
</Callout>
