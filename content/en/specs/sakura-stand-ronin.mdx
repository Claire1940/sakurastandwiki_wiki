<Section icon={Steps} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Ronin [Unawakened]">

Once you meet the prerequisites, you can begin the questline to unlock the Unawakened version of the **sakura stand ronin**. This process involves a complex sequence of puzzles, map navigation, and an initial boss fight.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Library Puzzle">
    Go to the Library and interact with the Strange Book on the desk. Agree to the prompt to receive a random 6-digit code (e.g., 365124). Interact with the bookcases from left to right in the exact order of the digits, then submit the code on the laptop to the right of the bookcases. Interact with the Strange Book again to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access the Back Alley">
    Locate the Back Alley entrance in the Dark Alley (across from Carmen). Find the small hole leading to a cave, and follow the path to the door leading to the Back Alley and Sakurian City. Keep heading right until you find another cave.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Find the Understrand Book">
    Inside the cave, continue heading right. When you encounter a spiderweb, turn right and then left to enter "The Understand". Interact with the Strange Book on the pedestal to receive a sequence of words. Screenshot or write down these words.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Chant at the Holy Grail">
    Head to the Holy Grail located in the middle of the Hall of Heroes. Stand inside the red circle and type the sequence of words you received into the chat, one word at a time. Once finished, click "Call Upon Chant" to summon the boss.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Defeat the Blade Within">
    Defeat the boss, **Ronin [The Blade Within]**, to complete the quest. Return to the Strange Book in The Understrand to officially unlock Ronin [Unawakened].
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Incantation Tip">
The words given by the Understrand book must be typed in chat exactly as they appeared, with correct capitalization and spelling, one word at a time, to trigger the boss fight.
</Callout>

</Section>

First, equip Ronin [Unawakened], return to the Library, and interact with the Strange Book to accept the "Heretic of Blades" quest. You must hunt down and defeat a player using one of several specific combat styles.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Ronin Boss Fight Strategies: Phase 1 & Phase 2">

Both boss fights in the **sakura stand ronin** questline are highly mechanical and punish aggressive button-mashing. Understanding the boss's attack patterns and knowing when to block or counter is key to survival.

| Boss Phase | Boss Name | Key Attack | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1** | The Blade Within | 100-Blade Spiral | Always block the spiral; do not attempt to parry |
| **Phase 2** | The Sword Saint | Teleport Slash | Let the teleport land, block immediately, then counter |

### Phase 1: The Blade Within Tactics
- **Avoid the Spiral**: The boss will throw out a massive barrage of blades followed by a five-slash combo. Keep your block up during this entire sequence.
- **Bait the R Move**: After the slashes, get close to the boss to bait their Ronin R move, then immediately dash away to avoid getting caught in it.
- **Execute Counter**: Once their R move finishes, execute your own Ronin R move to deal heavy damage. If you get hit during the setup, abort your combo and run away to reset.

### Phase 2: The Sword Saint Tactics
- **Teleport Baiting**: Let the Sword Saint succeed in using their teleport move on you. As soon as you see the boss disappear from your screen, immediately hold down your block button.
- **Punish Combo**: The moment the teleport attack ends, immediately retaliate with `M1x3, E, T, and R`. If landed correctly, this combo will deal massive damage without letting the boss recover.
- **Reset and Repeat**: Dash away immediately after the combo to avoid any counter-attacks, and wait for the boss to teleport again.

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Mastery">
Do not attempt to parry the spiral attacks in Phase 1. The multi-hit nature of the attack makes parrying highly inconsistent and will likely break your guard. Stick to standard blocking.
</Callout>

</Section>
