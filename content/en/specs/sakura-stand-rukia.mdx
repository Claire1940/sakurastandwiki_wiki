<Section icon={Swords} title="Rukia Moveset and Ability Breakdown">

The **sakura stand rukia** spec is heavily focused on ice manipulation, staying true to her character's origins. While the community has noted that her damage numbers are somewhat underwhelming compared to top-tier specs like Sukuna, her utility in team fights and 1v1s is undeniable due to the sheer amount of freezing CC she provides.

The moveset consists of six primary skills that revolve around stunning the opponent. Mastering the timing of these stuns is crucial because Rukia relies on "death by a thousand cuts" rather than massive burst damage.

| Key | Skill Name | Effect Description | Stun Potential |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Q** | Chilling Flourish | A rapid forward strike that stuns the target on impact. | High |
| **R** | Glacier Burst | Releases a burst of ice in a medium radius around the user. | Medium |
| **E** | Glacier Coasting | A mobility tool that leaves an icy trail, freezing caught enemies. | High |
| **H** | First Dance | A powerful ice-themed strike with significant knockback. | Low |
| **V** | Bankai | Activates the ultimate form, enhancing range and visuals. | Very High |
| **M1** | Basic Combo | A series of sword swings with decent speed but low damage. | None |

<Callout type="warning" title="Combat Warning">
Do not rely on Rukia for raw DPS. Her strength lies in keeping the opponent frozen. If you miss your CC skills, you will likely be out-damaged by aggressive specs.
</Callout>

Each skill has a specific role in a rotation. For instance, **Glacier Coasting** is excellent for closing the gap or escaping, while **Chilling Flourish** acts as the primary combo starter. In the current 2026 meta, players often use Rukia to peel for teammates in multi-player battles, where her freeze effects can set up high-damage allies for easy kills.
