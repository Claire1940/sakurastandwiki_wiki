<Section icon={Shield} title="Best Fight Plan and Combo Priorities">

Acerola is strongest when the match stays in your preferred distance band. You want to force movement with Z, tag with stack-building moves, and then spend H once the opponent is already uncomfortable. That flow keeps your opponent reacting instead of starting their own offense.

| Situation | Best Option | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Opening neutral | Z or T | Lets you enter space without overcommitting |
| Close-range scramble | E, R, or G | These hits convert well when the target is pinned |
| Opponent is retreating | Y or Z | Keeps chase pressure alive |
| After H lands | G or Y | Maximizes the amplified damage window |

| Practical Route | Purpose |
|---|---|
| T -> H -> G | Stack first, then spend the amplification on a heavy punish |
| Z -> Y -> E | Close distance and keep the opponent locked into your pace |
| E + Air -> R -> Y + Air | Mix vertical pressure with sustained contact |
