<Section icon={Map} title="How to Get Base Aizen in Sakura Stand">

To begin your journey toward unlocking the base **sakura stand aizen** spec, you must first transition into a Soul Reaper. This is achieved by defeating Hollows in Hueco Mundo while using the Demon Slayer Initiate spec, which grants a 20% chance to drop the Soul Reaper spec. Once you have secured the spec, travel to Las Noches and locate Aizen Sosuke to initiate his signature questline, "Never Give a Sword to a Man Who Can't Dance."

Obtaining each of the required swords represents a significant challenge, as they are tied to various bosses and rare drop tables across the map. For instance, you must defeat the formidable Toji Fushiguro boss to obtain the Split Soul Katana and conquer the Red Mist boss to secure Mimicry.

| Material | Quantity | Primary Acquisition Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Haunted Sword** | 1 | Found in boxes, chests, or item farming routes |
| **Split Soul Katana** | 1 | Obtained from defeating the Toji Fushiguro boss |
| **Tiantui Star's Blade** | 1 | Acquired via specialized progression routes |
| **Mimicry** | 1 | Dropped from defeating the Red Mist boss |
| **Nichirin Katana** | 1 | Standard starter katana spec reward |
| **Hogyoku Fragment** | 6 | Rare drops from Adjuchas, Gillians, or chests |
| **Cracked Mask** | 1 | Dropped by standard Hollows in Hueco Mundo |
| **Shattered Hollow Soul** | 15 | Common drop from normal Hollow enemies |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Deliver the Swords">
    Speak to Aizen in Las Noches while using Soul Reaper with Mastery 6. Hand over the five required swords in this exact order: Haunted Sword, Split Soul Katana, Tiantui Star's Blade, Mimicry, and Nichirin Katana.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Craft the Complete Hogyoku">
    Combine 5x Hogyoku Fragments, 1x Cracked Mask, and 5x Shattered Hollow Souls to create an Incomplete Hogyoku. Then, use 10x Shattered Hollow Souls on the Incomplete Hogyoku to craft the Complete Hogyoku Fragment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete Hunt Quest 1">
    Accept Aizen's next task: Kill 30 standard Hollows, 5 Menos Grande (Gillians), 2 Adjuchas, and 1 Vasto Lorde (the Barragan boss).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete Hunt Quest 2">
    Defeat a Starrk spec user in combat and defeat the Barragan boss once more to satisfy Aizen's demands.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Deliver the Final Fragment">
    Return to Aizen one last time and deliver a single Hogyoku Fragment to officially unlock the base Aizen spec.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Order Matters">
Handing over the five swords in any order other than the one specified by Aizen will cause the quest to fail, resulting in lost materials and progress.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Evolving to Hogyoku Fused Aizen">

Evolving Base Aizen into the feared Hogyoku Fused Aizen (commonly referred to as Aizen Evo) is an entirely different level of grind. This evolution requires you to interact with the Underworld Prince of Darkness NPC while actively using your base Aizen spec. This NPC is located deep within the Menos Forest, accessible via a black void portal in Hueco Mundo. 

The Underworld Prince of Darkness will test your resolve by tasking you with crafting highly complex items that require rare materials scattered throughout the game's various zones. Navigating these requirements requires careful planning and resource management.

| Target Item | Ingredient 1 | Ingredient 2 | Ingredient 3 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sakura King Curry Set** | 1x Gourmet Meal | 1x Amrita Drop | 1x Hogyoku Fragment |
| **Sugar Sapphire** | 1x Bari Bari Fruit | 2x Alternate Universe Honey | 3x Magic Ore |

To successfully craft these items, you must defeat specific bosses and farm unique environmental resources. For example, the Gourmet Meal requires materials dropped by the Gluten Curse boss. The Alternate Universe Honey must be harvested from beehives in the reset universe, while Magic Ore can be mined inside designated caves. The Bari Bari Devil Fruit can be found spawning randomly on the ground or obtained from chests.

<Callout type="success" title="Evolution Complete">
Once both the Sakura King Curry Set and the Sugar Sapphire are delivered, you will receive the True Hogyoku. Use it to immediately awaken your Aizen spec.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Moveset Mastery and Combat Strategy">

The combat style of Hogyoku Fused Aizen is defined by psychological warfare and high-risk, high-reward burst windows. By utilizing Kyoka Suigetsu, you can summon clones, disorient opponents, and turn completely invisible to set up devastating counter-attacks. Furthermore, Aizen possesses a unique execution mechanic that turns the tide of any battle instantly.

Understanding when to strike and when to fade into the shadows is the key to mastering this spec. Aizen's basic attacks flow seamlessly into his special abilities, allowing you to maintain constant pressure on your opponent.

| Key | Move Name | Damage Type | Combat Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **M1** | Basic Combo | Physical | Final hit disorients targets and breaks guard |
| **Q** | Kyoka Suigetsu | Illusion | Summons clones, inflicts silence, grants invisibility |
| **T** | True Power | Execution | Triggers execution cutscene if target is below 20% HP |
| **F** | Teleport Counter | Mobility | Teleports behind the target's back to punish attacks |

Your primary goal in combat is to deal consistent damage to charge your Awakening meter. Once you deal 600 damage (or 500 damage if you have unlocked the "Treachery" title), Aizen will enter his fused state. In this mode, your damage output is boosted by 30%, but you will also take 60% more damage from all sources. This makes the mode incredibly lethal for both you and your opponent, requiring precise timing and flawless execution.

<Callout type="info" title="Awakening Mode">
While in Awakening, your defense is heavily compromised. Avoid reckless trades and rely on your Teleport Counter (F) to avoid high-damage enemy combos.
</Callout>

</Section>
