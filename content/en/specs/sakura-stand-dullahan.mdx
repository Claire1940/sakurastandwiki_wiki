<Section icon={Target} title="How to Obtain Dullahan: Step-by-Step Guide">

Securing the **Sakura Stand Dullahan** spec requires a specific sequence of actions. You cannot roll this ability from a standard Stand Arrow; instead, it belongs to the "Spec" category which necessitates item-based progression.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Achieve Standless State">
    Use a **Rokakaka Fruit** to reset your current ability. You must be in the **Standless** base state to consume the Remembrance of Dullahan. You can purchase Rokakaka from Auddy's Shop or find them in spawned boxes across the map.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm for Remembrance of Dullahan">
    The core item, **Remembrance of Dullahan**, is a rare drop found in **Boxes** or obtained through specific **Seasonal Events**. In the current 2026 update cycle, players have reported higher drop rates during nighttime in-game cycles or through high-tier chest farming routes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Verify Mastery Level">
    Open your menu (M) and check your Mastery. Ensure you are at least **Mastery 1**. If you are below this, farm NPCs like the Thugs or the Zen'in Clan members to quickly gain the necessary experience.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Consume the Item">
    With the item in your inventory and your character in the Standless state, select the **Remembrance of Dullahan** and use it. Your character will undergo a visual transformation, signifying the activation of the Dullahan spec.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Item Loss Warning">
If you use a Rokakaka Fruit *after* obtaining Dullahan, you will lose the spec and return to Standless. You will need another Remembrance of Dullahan item to get it back. Always double-check before resetting.
</Callout>
