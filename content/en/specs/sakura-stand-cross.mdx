<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Obtainment Guide">

Acquiring **sakura stand cross** involves a multi-stage soul-hunting process. Players must gather two distinct soul fragments and combine them under specific conditions. Note that since this is a limited spec, if the event is not active, these items must be acquired through player-to-player trading.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire the Half Human Soul">
    The Half Human Soul is obtained by defeating other players in PvP. When an opponent is defeated, there is a random chance for a soul fragment to hover over their body. You must manually walk over and collect it to add it to your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm the Half Monster Soul">
    This is the most difficult part of the process. You must locate and defeat the **Silver Chariot Requiem** boss. This boss typically spawns every hour. The drop rate for the Half Monster Soul is approximately **13% (1 out of 12 chance)**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prepare the Standless State">
    Before combining the souls, you must ensure your character is **Standless**. Using the souls while having another stand or spec active will result in failure and the souls will not activate. Use a Rokakaka if necessary to clear your current slot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Combine and Transform">
    Place both the Half Human Soul and the Half Monster Soul in your toolbar. Select either soul and use it. They will automatically merge into the **Human & Monster Soul**. Use this final item while Standless to permanently unlock Cross.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Soul Component | Source | Drop Rate |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Half Human Soul** | Players (PvP) | Chance-based |
| **Half Monster Soul** | Chariot Requiem Boss | ~13% (1/12) |
| **Merged Soul** | Inventory Crafting | 100% (Upon usage) |

<Callout type="warning" title="Requirement Check">
Always verify you are in the Standless state before clicking the Human & Monster Soul. Failing to do so is a common mistake that prevents the spec from binding to your character.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Phase 2: X!Gaster (Take Control)">

By pressing the **H** key, the user allows X!Gaster to take control. This transforms the character into a more aggressive state with enhanced bone magic and faster cooldowns. However, this power comes at a significant cost: your health will drain by 2 HP per second until you reach 75 HP.

| Key | Move Name | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **E** | **Frenzy Slash** | A multi-hit dashing slash that deals wide AOE damage. |
| **R** | **Overwrite Bones** | Unleashes waves of bones in a straight line with 1s stun. |
| **T** | **Overwrite Blaster** | A faster version of the standard blaster with a larger AOE. |
| **Y** | **Down Lock** | A counter move. Teleports behind the attacker for two upward slashes. |
| **G** | **Raging Smite** | Spinning knife dash leading into a cinematic slashing execution. |

<Callout type="info" title="Transformation Mechanics">
The health drain from Phase 2 cannot kill you. It will stop exactly at **75 HP**. This makes Phase 2 a "glass cannon" mode where you must finish the fight quickly before your low health makes you vulnerable to a single combo.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="PvP Strategy and Best Practices">

To master **sakura stand cross** in 2026, you must learn to manage the sluggish startup of your heavy attacks. Success with this spec relies on "baiting" and utilizing your I-frames effectively.

**Optimal Combo Chain:**
1. Start with **Z (Teleport)** to close the distance.
2. Land **E (Corruption Strike)** for the 5-second stun.
3. Follow up with **M1 x3**.
4. Use **T (X!Blaster)** to pull them back as they try to escape.
5. Finish with **G (I AM THE X EVENT)** for massive cinematic damage.

| Matchup Type | Difficulty | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Close Range** | Easy | Use E-Stun and M1 trades. |
| **Long Range** | Moderate | Rely on Z-Teleport and T-Blaster pulls. |
| **High Mobility** | Hard | Wait for them to commit, then use Q I-frames to punish. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Mastering the Counter">
