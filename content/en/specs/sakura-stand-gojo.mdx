<Section icon={Target} title="Requirements and Spawn Mechanics">

Before you can even think about wielding the Power of Six Eyes, you must satisfy several prerequisites. The most important is possessing the Yuji Itadori Spec. The quest for Gojo is essentially an evolution of Yuji's journey, reflecting the narrative of collecting Sukuna's fingers to manage the cursed energy within.

| Requirement | Detail | Importance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Base Spec** | Yuji Itadori must be equipped | Mandatory |
| **Quest Item** | 20 Sukuna Fingers | Mandatory |
| **Player Count** | 5+ players required in server | Mandatory |
| **Spawn Timer** | Every 5 minutes | High |
| **Detection Skill** | Cursed Energy Detection (Q) | High |

The spawning of Sukuna Fingers is tied to a specific server mechanic. Every five minutes, the game performs a check. If there are at least five players in the server, there is a 50% chance (1 out of 2) that a "Second Vessel" will spawn. This vessel is not an NPC but is actually "bonded" to a random player in the server.

<Callout type="warning" title="Server Constraints">
Grinding for Gojo in a private server alone is impossible unless you have at least four alts or friends joining you. The "Second Vessel" will not spawn if the player count is below five.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Obtaining Gojo">

The path to obtaining Gojo is a marathon, not a sprint. You will need to actively hunt players who have been marked as Vessels. This requires constant vigilance and the strategic use of Yuji's built-in detection tools.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accept the Quest">
    Locate the Gojo NPC in the main map area. While equipped with the Yuji Itadori Spec, interact with him to accept the quest to collect 20 Sukuna Fingers. He will acknowledge the cursed energy within you and set you on your path.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Monitor the Timer">
    Stay in a server with at least five players. Every five minutes, use your **Cursed Energy Detection (Q skill)**. If a Vessel has spawned, a player in the server will glow with a distinct **red aura**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat the Vessel">
    Once you identify the player marked as the Vessel, you must defeat them in combat. Upon their "death," they will drop a Sukuna Finger. Note that this can be difficult in public servers where other players might try to steal the kill or the drop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect 20 Fingers">
    Repeat the hunting process until you have 20 Sukuna Fingers in your inventory. You can track your progress by talking to the Gojo NPC or checking your quest log. This process typically takes several hours depending on your luck with the 50% spawn chance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Final Exchange">
    With all 20 fingers collected, return to the Gojo NPC. He will perform the final ritual, removing the Yuji Itadori Spec from your character and replacing it with the permanent Gojo Spec.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Tip">
If you are struggling to find Vessels in a public server, coordinate with a group of friends in a private server. As long as you maintain five players, you can take turns being the Vessel and helping each other collect fingers quickly.
</Callout>

| Activity | Time Investment | Difficulty | Reward |

| **Vessel Hunting** | 2-4 Hours | Medium | Sukuna Fingers |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Gojo Move Set and Combat Analysis">

Gojo's power is divided into two states: **Masked** (Blindfold on) and **Unmasked** (Blindfold off). You toggle between these using the **Q key**. Each mode offers a different tactical advantage, though the Unmasked mode is generally where his most devastating "Maximum Output" moves reside.

  </Card>
  <Card title="Unmasked Mode (Aggressive)">

<Callout type="info" title="The Power of Hollow Purple">
Hollow Purple is a "traveling" move. It doesn't just hit the area in front of you; it moves across the map until it hits a solid object or reaches its maximum range, hitting everything in its path.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Combat Strategies">

Mastering Gojo requires understanding the synergy between **Lapse Blue** and **Reversal Red**. These moves are the bread and butter of your combo game. A common strategy is to use Lapse Blue (Y) to pull an escaping enemy into your range, followed immediately by an M1 combo, and finishing with Reversal Red (T) to reset the neutral game.

**PvP vs. PvE Performance:**
- **PvE**: Gojo is an S-tier farmer. His AOE moves like Hollow Purple can clear entire waves of NPCs instantly.
- **PvP**: Gojo is a "Hit and Run" specialist. Because his animations (especially for Hollow Purple and Domain Expansion) are long, he is vulnerable to being interrupted. You must time your stuns perfectly.
