<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Progression and Core Gameplay">

Starting your journey in Sakura Stand can be overwhelming due to the sheer variety of combat paths. Every player begins as "Standless," a base state that is actually required for many of the game's most powerful quest-based Specs. The core gameplay loop involves fighting enemies to gain Mastery and XP, which then allows you to equip stronger abilities.

For new players, the first goal should be obtaining a basic Stand or a simple Spec to make farming easier. While the temptation to go for a "God Tier" ability like Cid Kagenou or Sukuna is high, these require significant Mastery levels and rare items. It is better to master a reliable "Arrow Stand" first.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Resource Gathering">
    Scavenge the map for Boxes and Chests to find your first Stand Arrow and Rokakaka Fruit. These items are the foundation of early-game progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Rolling Your First Stand">
    Use a Stand Arrow while in the Standless state. This will grant you a Stand from the common pool, such as Star Platinum or Crazy Diamond, providing you with your first combat move-set.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mastery and XP Farming">
    Engage in combat with NPCs to raise your Mastery level. Remember that Roblox Premium users enjoy a 1.15x XP multiplier, which is highly beneficial for reaching the Mastery 1 and Mastery 6 gates.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilizing Active Codes">
    Open the in-game menu, navigate to Settings, and enter active codes like "RTZREWORK" or "JOJOMONTH" to receive free Tokens, Cash, and EXP boosts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Choosing a Specialization">
    Decide whether you want to follow the Stand Evolution path (like Made in Heaven) or the Spec Quest path (like the Jujutsu Kaisen or Cyberpunk routes).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Advice">
Do not use your Rokakaka Fruit prematurely. Only use it when you are ready to switch to a specific quest-based Spec or if you rolled a Stand you truly do not enjoy.
</Callout>

</Section>
