<Section icon={Swords} title="Choso and Naoya Boss Fight Strategies">

The final hurdle to obtaining Naoya Zen'in involves two major boss battles: Choso and Naoya himself. Understanding their mechanics is crucial, as a single mistake can reset your progress in the dungeon raid.

### Choso Boss Fight
Choso spawns in the Silver Chariot Requiem tunnel every 1 hour and 30 minutes. He uses powerful blood manipulation techniques, including long-range blood shots and stun-heavy combos. Defeating him requires constant dodging and picking your windows of opportunity. While he has a 25% chance to drop the Death Painting (used to unlock the Choso spec), you only need to defeat him once to progress your Naoya quest.

### Naoya Boss Dungeon Raid
The Naoya boss raid is a solo dungeon accessed by speaking to Naobito after completing the previous eight stages. This is an endurance run where Naoya utilizes his signature Projection Sorcery, dashing at extreme speeds and delivering rapid-fire combos.
