<Section icon={Search} title="Phase 1: The Perception Blocking Mask">

The first major hurdle in the **sakura stand roland quest** is obtaining the Perception Blocking Mask. This item does not drop from a standard boss but relies on a specific server mechanic tied to player activity.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Speak to Olivia">
    Locate Olivia in the Alleyway. He will mention a mysterious library and task you with finding a "Black Mask" to prove your worth.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check Server Count">
    Ensure there are at least 10 players in your current server. The mask will not spawn in low-population or private servers with fewer than 10 people.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Hunt for the Drop">
    Every 10 minutes, there is a random chance for one player in the server to "drop" the mask upon being killed. You can use your Q skill on NPCs or players to check if the drop is active (indicated by a black aura/mask icon).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Verification">
    Once you obtain the item, it will appear in your inventory as the **Perception Blocking Mask**. Return to Olivia immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Server Hopping">
Do not server hop frequently. The 10-minute timer resets or may be inconsistent across different servers. It is better to stay in a stable, populated server and participate in combat.
</Callout>

| **Total Damage** | 500 Points | Use heavy attacks and combos on high-HP targets |

Upon completion, the NPC rewards you with the **Smoldering Fragment**. This fragment is the key to entering the Library's inner sanctum.

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Efficiency">

<Section icon={Map} title="Solving the Library Color Puzzle">

The final barrier in the **sakura stand roland quest** is the Librarian's laptop puzzle. The Librarian provides a sequence of colors: **Red, Green, Orange, White, Blue, Purple**. You must translate these colors into a numeric code by visiting specific locations across the map.

| Color | Location to Check | Example Number |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **White** | Library Interior | 1 |
| **Purple** | Hall of Heroes | 2 |
| **Blue** | Noob Area | 3 |
| **Green** | Ghetto / Alleyway | 4 |
| **Orange** | Cafe / Central | 5 |
| **Red** | Hueco Mundo | 6 |

**How to Solve:**
1. Visit each location listed above.
2. Look for a hidden number etched into the environment or near the entrance.
3. Match the number to the color assigned to that location.
4. Enter the sequence into the laptop in the order: **Red -> Green -> Orange -> White -> Blue -> Purple**.

<Callout type="warning" title="Color Blindness Alert">
The Blue and White colors can look similar in certain lighting. Double-check the location names to ensure you are attributing the correct number to the correct color in the sequence.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Unlocking Roland: The Black Silence Mechanics">

Once the puzzle is solved and weapons are collected, you receive the **Library Invitation**. Take this back to Olivia in the Alleyway. He will exchange the invitation for the **Gloves of Black Silence**, officially completing the **sakura stand roland quest**.

Roland is a unique spec that features three distinct phases that evolve during combat based on your hit count.

| Phase | Activation | Skill Changes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1** | Initial State | Basic weapon slashes and gun skills (Q/Y) |
| **Phase 2** | Low Hit Count | Increased range on Y skill and faster combos |
| **Phase 3** | High Hit Count | E skill gains falling knives; G skill adds a final slash |

**Skill Overview:**
- **Q Skill**: A multi-hit weapon cycle involving swords and guns.
- **Y Skill**: A workshop-based strike that changes properties per phase.
- **H Skill**: A cinematic "Cutscene" move that deals massive AOE damage.
- **Z Skill**: Defensive paintbrush maneuver to parry or reposition.

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Combat Tip">
Roland excels in "pressure" gameplay. By constantly landing hits, you maintain Phase 3, which provides the largest hitboxes and highest damage output in the game.
</Callout>
