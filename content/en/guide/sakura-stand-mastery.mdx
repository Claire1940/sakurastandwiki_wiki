<Section icon={Map} title="Optimized Leveling Routes for 2026">

Reaching high levels of **sakura stand mastery** requires a strategic approach to the map and its various encounters. New players often make the mistake of staying in the starting areas for too long, where XP gains diminish quickly. To reach Mastery 6 and beyond, you must follow a structured path that challenges your current build while rewarding you with substantial experience.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Standless Foundation">
    Start as Standless and farm basic enemies until you reach Mastery 1. Use your first Stand Arrow to roll for a reliable combat Stand like Star Platinum or Crazy Diamond. This will make your initial farming much faster than staying Standless.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="JJK and Cyberware Grinding">
    Once you hit Mastery 1, transition to the Jujutsu Kaisen or Sandevistan routes. Farming Cursed Spirits or collecting Cyberware parts provides a steady stream of XP while you work toward high-tier items like Sukuna's Fingers or the Cyan Cyberdeck.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Boss Rotation and Mastery 3">
    At Mastery 3, you should begin a boss rotation. Focus on the Deku boss in the Ruined City or Toji Fushiguro. These encounters offer massive XP chunks. If you are struggling, join a group in the official Discord to farm bosses collectively.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Mastery 6 Push">
    The final stretch to Mastery 6 is best handled in the Hueco Mundo route or the Library. Farming Adjuchas and Gillians for Hogyoku Fragments provides the density of enemies needed to push through the final levels required for the Roland questline.
  </Step>
</Steps>
