<Section icon={Target} title="Core Progression Steps">

Progression in this experience is not just about raw power; it is about choosing a path that matches your current skill level and resource availability. Follow these steps to build a consistent character.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Assessment">
    Launch the game and prioritize learning the basic controls. Avoid jumping into complex boss fights immediately; spend time mastering your first Stand's moveset on weaker NPCs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Ability Selection">
    Decide between a Stand (Arrow-based) or a Spec (Manual/Quest-based). Stands are generally faster to acquire for beginners, while Specs offer deeper, quest-heavy gameplay.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Resource Farming">
    Collect Tokens and Cash by participating in events and fighting enemies. Use these resources to purchase items from the shop that facilitate your chosen ability route.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Questing and Bosses">
    Once your Mastery level is sufficient, tackle specific NPC questlines. Use the official Discord to track current event objectives and limited-time rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Warning">
The game includes full-bright flashing lights and intense visual effects. If you are sensitive to such stimuli, adjust your graphics settings immediately to ensure a safe and comfortable experience.
</Callout>
