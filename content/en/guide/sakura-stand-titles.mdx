<Section icon={Trophy} title="Breakthrough & Mastery Title Progression">

Unlocking the most prestigious **sakura stand titles** requires a deep investment into the Mastery and Breakthrough systems. These are not earned through luck, but through consistent gameplay and power scaling.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Mastery Leveling">
    Focus on leveling your character's Mastery. At Mastery 2, 3, 5, and 14, you unlock fundamental buff titles like Above All and Shielded Warrior. These provide the baseline stats needed for higher-tier content.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The First Breakthrough">
    Once you hit the level cap, perform your first Breakthrough. This resets your level but grants you the 'Breakthrough' title and your first Breakthrough Point, which can be spent on economic titles like 'The Rich'.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Climbing the Ranks">
    Reach Breakthrough 3 to unlock **Beyond** and Breakthrough 5 for **Infinity**. These titles signal to other players that you have successfully completed the leveling loop multiple times.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Grind to Fragmented">
    Breakthrough 10 is the current 'soft cap' for prestige. Unlocking the **Fragmented** title requires significant time investment but offers the most distinct visual glitch effect in the game.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Mastery 15 | **Mastered** | Vanity |
