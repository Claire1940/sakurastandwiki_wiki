<Section icon={Swords} title="Keyboard Controls & Posing Keybinds">

While chat emotes require typing, character poses are much more accessible and are designed to be used seamlessly during gameplay. Posing is a core mechanic of the JoJo-inspired aesthetic, allowing your character and their active Stand to strike a dramatic, high-contrast stance.

Understanding how to integrate posing with your basic movement controls is essential for maintaining fluid gameplay. Below is a breakdown of the primary keyboard controls in Sakura Stand, showing how the pose keybind fits into the overall layout.

| Control Key | In-Game Action | Gameplay Function & Tactical Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **WASD** | Character Movement | Move your character and immediately cancel active poses or emotes |
| **W + W** | Sprinting | Double-tap W to run faster across the map or escape danger |

| **Double Space** | Double Jump | Jump higher to reach elevated platforms or dodge ground attacks |
| **C Key** | Dashing | Perform a quick directional dash to reposition during combat |
| **P Key** | Posing | Strike your Stand or Spec's signature pose and play its theme |
