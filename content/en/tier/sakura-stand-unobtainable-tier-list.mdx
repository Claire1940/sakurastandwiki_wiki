<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Rarity Factors and Spec Maintenance">

Why do certain specs stay at the top of the **sakura stand unobtainable tier list**? It comes down to three factors: **Drop Rate**, **Quest Complexity**, and **Copyright Changes**.

1. **Drop Rate**: Items like the "Heavenly Restriction" aura for Toji have a 25% drop rate, but the boss himself is difficult to spawn. When these mechanics are removed, the difficulty is "locked in."
2. **Quest Complexity**: Made in Heaven (MiH) required a "New Moon" in real life. This 13-day waiting period limited the number of players who could complete the quest before it was cycled out.
3. **Copyright**: When a spec like Deku is changed to Green Board, the original "un-reworked" versions sometimes become legacy items with unique IDs, increasing their value to collectors.
