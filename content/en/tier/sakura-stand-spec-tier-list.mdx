<Section icon={Shield} title="Strategic Matchups and Counter-Play">

Even the best spec on the **sakura stand spec tier list** has weaknesses. PVP in Sakura Stand is a game of "bait and punish." Knowing which specs counter others can save you from losing bounty or ranking points.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Zoning vs. Rushdown">

<Callout type="info" title="Combat Note">
Many newer specs feature **Domain Expansions**. These are high-risk, high-reward moves that trap opponents. If you see a domain forming, prioritize your escape or defensive cooldowns immediately.
